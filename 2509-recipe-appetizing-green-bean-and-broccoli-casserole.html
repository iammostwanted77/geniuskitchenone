Ingredients of Green Bean and Broccoli Casserole

  1. Prepare 10 3/4 oz of Cream of Mushroom Soup.
  2. You need 3/4 cup of Milk.
  3. Prepare 1/4 tsp of Pepper.
  4. You need 9 oz of Green Beans.
  5. It's 1 1/3 cup of Cheddar French Fried Onions.
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup of Bacon (chopped).
  7. Prepare 9 oz of Broccoli.
  8. It's 1/2 cup of Cheese (shredded).

Green Bean and Broccoli Casserole instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix cream of mushroom soup, milk and pepper in a 1 1/2 quart baking dish.
  3. Stir in grean beans, broccoli, bacon, 1/4 cup cheese, and 2/3 cup french fried onions.
  4. Bake for 30min. or until hot.
  5. Top with remaining 2/3 cup french fried onions and 1/4 cup cheese, and bake additional 5min. or until onions are golden.
