Ingredients of Crock Pot Taco Meat

  1. It's 2 pounds of meat – ground beef.
  2. You need 1 cup of salsa (per every 2 pds of meat.).
  3. You need 4 tablespoons of Taco seasoning (see my taco seasoning recipe) (2 tablespoon per every 2 pds of meat.).

Crock Pot Taco Meat instructions

  1. Place the ground meat (raw) in the crock pot..
  2. Pour the salsa on top and sprinkle with the taco seasoning mix..
  3. Cover and cook on high for 3 -4 hours until no longer pink. Use a spoon to break up the taco meat and serve. (drain any fat off the meat).
