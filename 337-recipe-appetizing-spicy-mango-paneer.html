Spicy Mango Paneer.

You can cook Spicy Mango Paneer using 20 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Spicy Mango Paneer

  1. You need 5-6 of mangoes.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of paneer.
  3. You need 1 of medium size onion cut into small cubes.
  4. It's 1 of capsicum.
  5. It's 1 tsp of finely chopped garlic.
  6. It's 2 tsp of finely chopped green chillies.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of oil.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of chilli garlic sauce.
  9. Prepare 2 tsp of honey.
  10. Prepare 1 tsp of black salt.
  11. Prepare Bunch of green onions.
  12. Prepare 2 tsp of roasted sesame seeds.
  13. You need of For marinate the paneer.
  14. You need 2 tsp of corn flour.
  15. It's 1 tsp of ginger paste.
  16. You need to taste of Salt.
  17. You need 1 tsp of garam masala powder.
  18. It's 1 tsp of dry mango powder.
  19. You need 1 tsp of green chilli paste.
  20. It's 1 tsp of lemon juice.

Spicy Mango Paneer instructions

  1. First roughly chopped the mangoes then in a blender,add mangoes,dry red chilli,garlic don’t add water blend it to a fine paste, now heat a kadai add the prepared mango puree stir it then add chilli garlic paste,honey cook it for 2 minutes.
  2. Then turn off the heat once done keep it aside now in a mixing bowl add the paneer cubes and other spices and corn flour mix it keep it rest for 10 minutes then deep fry those paneer cubes,now heat a kadai add oil add chopped garlic sauté it for 1 minutes.
  3. Then add onion cubes sauté it for few minutes then add fried paneer cubes add the prepared mango puree toss it put some green onions mix it turn off the heat garnish it with some ripe chopped mangoes roasted sesame seeds......
  4. Ready to serve..
