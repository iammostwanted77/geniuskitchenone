Horchata step by step

  1. Heat water, sugar, and cinnamon in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Simmer until sugar is dissolved (it might take a few minutes.).
  3. Remove mixture from heat and allow to cool..
  4. Pour rice milk into a pitcher, or any container that is large enough to hold 1 quart and 1/3 cup of liquid.
  5. Pour the sugar/water/cinnamon mixture into the rice milk and stir.
  6. Let chill in the fridge.
  7. Tip: Try not to add ice because when the ice melts, it gets pretty watered down.
  8. Optional: Add tapioca balls if you want. It makes it taste really good!.
