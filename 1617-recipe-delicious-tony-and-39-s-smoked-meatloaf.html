Tony's smoked meatloaf instructions

  1. Mix all your ingredients together except: breadcrumbs, eggs and liquid smoke. I used a sheet pan it made it easier for me to form my loafs..
  2. In a small bowl mix together your breadcrumbs, eggs and liquid smoke. Add this to your meat mixture and form into your loafs..
  3. After your loafs are formed and tuck tightly, place the meatloaf on a very hot and ready smoker..
  4. After your meatloaf has been on the smoker for about 30 minutes, flip it over and start basin it (every 30 mins) with the barbecue sauce of your choice it should take approximately 1 1/2 to 2 hours to smoke.(depending on the size of your meatloaf).
  5. You can add more or less of the ingredients I provided, It all depends on what you like and desire..
