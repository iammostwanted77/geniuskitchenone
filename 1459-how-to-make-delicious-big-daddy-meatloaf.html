Big Daddy Meatloaf step by step

  1. In a large mixing bowl, mix in the garlic and onion..
  2. Mix in everything else. Mix it in well. If it doesnt form correctly, then add more breadcrumbs..
  3. Place meat mixture in a meatloaf pan. Cover the inside with EVOO..
  4. Preheat oven 350 degrees..
  5. Place the loaf in the center of the oven and bake for 1 hour and 10 mins..
