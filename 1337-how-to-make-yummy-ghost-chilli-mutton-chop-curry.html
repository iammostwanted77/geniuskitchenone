Ghost chilli mutton chop curry step by step

  1. Marinate the mutton chops in the marinademix for 5-6 hours or overnight preferably..
  2. In a heavy bottomed non stick panheat oil.Add tomato paste and kashmiri red chilli powder. Saute on low heat for a minute..
  3. Add the chops along with the marinade;saute till the oil starts to separate..
  4. Add water and salt..
  5. Cover with a tight fitted lid and let cook on simmer till the oil floats on top and the chops are cooked through and tender..
  6. Add garam masala powder and stir through..
  7. Serve hot with rice..
