  1. Place spaghetti squash in a large microwave safe bowl. Reheat if needed and cover to keep warm. Note: I purchase medium sized squash. Cut lengthwise in half. Remove seeds, etc. Spray with non-stick spray, salt/pepper. Turn rind side up. Sprinkle olive oil over rind, brush/cover rind with oil. Place on pan/cookie sheet lined with foil. Bake at 425 degrees for 30-35 minutes. Check softness by pressing with paper towel. If rind softened remove from oven and scrap spaghetti with fork into bowl. Don't over cook.   2. In a medium bowl combine crushed tomatoes, Parmesan, basil, Italian seasoning and salt. Mix well. Note: I adjusted the Italian seasoning to just shy of 1 Tbsp the second time I prepared. Initially it was too bland..

  4. Add garlic to skillet. Cook and stir until veggies have softened and garlic is fragrant, about 2 minutes..
