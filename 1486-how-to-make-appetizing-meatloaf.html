Meatloaf step by step

  1. Pre heat oven 350. In a large bowl add all ingredients, saving the hamburger meat for last. Mix throughly with your hands..
  2. Get a pan for the oven and grease before placing the meatloaf in..
  3. In a small mixing bowl add ingredients for sauce. Stir sauce until mixed and then pour on top of meat loaf. Place in the oven. At 1 hour I checked needed a little more time so went 20 minutes longer..
  4. Extra Sauce: I made an extra of sauce and cooked on stove. Brought to a boil string make sure does not burn, then low heat until was ready for it. Added to meatloaf, because my boy's like extra sauce. Enjoy.....
