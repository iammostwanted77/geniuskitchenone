Keto Beef Stroganoff Slow Cooker Freezer Meal instructions

  1. Prepair low carb noodle option the night before and refrigerate.
  2. Thaw ingredients in refrigerator overnight.
  3. Place thawed ingredients in slow cooker on low for 8hrs.
  4. When ready to eat add sour cream and stir until incorporated.
  5. Reheat low carb option.
  6. Serve over low carb option.
