Ingredients of Baked Alaska Meat Loaf

  1. It's 1 1/2 lb of ground beef.
  2. Prepare 1 of brown gravy mix.
  3. It's 2 tsp of minced onions.
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp of ground black pepper.
  5. Prepare 1 envelope of instant mashed potato (4 serving).
  6. You need 1 tsp of chives (optional).
  7. You need 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese.

Baked Alaska Meat Loaf instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350° F..
  2. In a large bowl mix ground beef, gravy mix, minced onion, and pepper together..
  3. Shape into a loaf, 2 inches high, in a shallow baking pan..
  4. Stir chives into prepared mashed potatoes..
  5. Evenly spread over meat loaf. If you don't pat the potato on well enough some may slide off during cooking..
  6. Bake 55 minutes or until cooked through..
  7. Remove from oven and sprinkle with cheese. Bake 2 minutes longer or until cheese melts..
  8. Serve & enjoy :).
