Ingredients of Soft Taco Brunch

  1. It's 1/4 pound of reshoots sausage.
  2. You need 4 of flour tortillas 6 inch.
  3. Prepare 4 of large eggs.
  4. You need 6 tablespoons of sour cream divided.
  5. You need 2 tablespoons of butter.
  6. Prepare To taste of salt and pepper.

Soft Taco Brunch step by step

  1. Melt the butter. Scramble the eggs and season with salt and pepper..
  2. Remove the casing from the reshoots. Slice and add to a hot skillet. As it cooks chop the sausage pieces into bits..
  3. Heat the tortillas till crispy. Add 1-1/2 tablespoons of sour cream on each tortilla. Speard the sour cream out..
  4. Add the eggs and sausage to the tortillas. Serve I hope you enjoy!!.
