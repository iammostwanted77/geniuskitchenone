Diabolic Deviled Eggs step by step

  1. Cut the eggs in half lengthwise, place the yolks in a small mixing bowl, and set the whites aside..
  2. Add the scallion, cilantro, cumin, parsley, pepper, ranch dressing, mustard, and chipotle to the egg yolks and mix well with a fork until smooth..
  3. Season with salt to taste..
  4. Spoon the yolk mixture into the hollow part of each egg white..
  5. There should be enough yolk filling to form a small dome on top of each egg white..
  6. Place in refrigerator and serve when cool..
