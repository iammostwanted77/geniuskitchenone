Ingredients of Cucumber and Spaghetti Squash Salad

  1. It's 2 cups of spaghetti squash, cooked.
  2. You need 1/2 of cucumber, thinly sliced.
  3. You need 1/2 of chicken breast, boiled.
  4. Prepare 3 Tbsp of vinegar.
  5. Prepare 3 Tbsp of soy sauce.
  6. You need 1/2 Tbsp of sesame oil.
