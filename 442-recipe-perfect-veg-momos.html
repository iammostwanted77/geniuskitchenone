Ingredients of Veg Momos

  1. Prepare 150 gm of Maida.
  2. It's 100 gm of cabbage.
  3. Prepare 1/2 of carrot, grated.
  4. Prepare 1 of Small Onion, Chopped.
  5. It's 1/3 cup of Spring Onion.
  6. It's 1/2 tsp of Black Pepper Powder.
  7. You need 1 tsp of Soy Sauce.
  8. It's of Salt as per taste.
  9. You need 2 of Green Chilli, chopped.
  10. You need 4 of Garlic Cloves, Chopped.
  11. It's of Oil For Deep Frying.

Veg Momos step by step

  1. Mix maida and salt. Add few drops of oil and combine well..
  2. Add water little at a time and start kneading the dough..
  3. Make a smooth dough, cover and rest for 15 minutes..
  4. Heat oil in a pan..
  5. Add garlic cloves and green chili to the pan. Saute for couple of minutes..
  6. Add chopped onion and saute for 2 minutes..
  7. Add carrot and cabbage. Mix properly and fry for another minute..
  8. Add spring onion, fry for 2 seconds and turn off the gas..
  9. Let the mixture cool..
  10. Once it is cool, add soy sauce, salt and black pepper powder. Mix well..
  11. Transfer the mixture to the bowl..
  12. Stuffing is ready..
  13. Take out the dough. Knead it again and make small balls out of it..
  14. Take one ball and roll it like a puri..
  15. Put 2 tsp the stuffing at the center..
  16. Apply water at the edges and fold it like a Modak..
  17. Likewise, make all the momos..
  18. Now we need to steam the momos..
  19. Grease a pan with some oil..
  20. Place all the momos in this pan..
  21. Pout Idli Pot with enough water at the base..
  22. When water starts to boil, keep the momos pan inside Idli pot carefully..
  23. Cover the pot and let it cook for 10 minutes..
  24. Momos are ready to serve..
