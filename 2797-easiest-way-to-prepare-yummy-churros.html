Churros instructions

  1. Boil the water in a pan..
  2. While boiling, add the butter, brown sugar and salt. Stir thoroughly..
  3. After being mixed, pour the all purpose flour. Mix thoroughly..
  4. Add 1 egg in the mixture then mix. Add the other egg then mix..
  5. Preheat the oil before frying the mixture. Only in a low heat so the churros will be fried evenly and not burnt. You can pipe in directly the mixture in the pan using a piping bag with a big star shaped tip..
  6. In a bowl, mix the white sugar and cinnamon powder. Then roll the churros in the mixture..
  7. Boil the all purpose cream in a pan on a medium heat..
  8. Put the chocolate in a bowl. Then pour the all purpose cream..
