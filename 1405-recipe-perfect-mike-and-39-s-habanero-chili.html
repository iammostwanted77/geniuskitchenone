Ingredients of Mike's Habanero Chili

  1. Prepare 1.5 lbs of Ground Beef.
  2. Prepare 1 tbs of Olive Oil.
  3. You need 1 of med Yellow Onion, diced.
  4. You need 5-6 cloves of Garlic, minced.
  5. It's 1/2 of large Green Bell Pepper, chopped.
  6. You need 2-3 of large Carrots, peeled and chopped.
  7. It's 1-2 stalks of Celery, chopped.
  8. You need 2 of Habaneros, diced (reduce to 1 if you are sensitive to extreme heat).
  9. You need 1 (15 oz) of can Tomato Sauce.
  10. It's 2 (14.5 oz) of cans Fire Roasted Diced Tomatoes (juice included).
  11. You need 1 (14.5 oz) of can Pinto Beans (not drained).
  12. You need 2 tbs of Chili Powder.
  13. Prepare 1 tbs of *Spiceologist Smokey Habanero Rub (www.spiceologist.com).
  14. Prepare 3 tsp of Oregano (Mexican Oregano Preferred but not necessary).
  15. You need 3 tsp of Cumin.
  16. Prepare 2 tsp of Sea Salt (adjust to taste).
  17. You need 2 tsp of Paprika.
  18. It's 2 tsp of Garlic Powder.
  19. It's of Bacon (crumbled for topping).
  20. You need of Shredded Sharp Cheddar (for topping).

Mike's Habanero Chili step by step

  1. Add Olive Oil to skillet on medium heat. Add chopped Onion and Garlic and saute for 4-5 mins (until onions are translucent and the garlic starts to brown)..
  2. Add the Ground Beef and brown. Drain fat..
  3. In a Crock Pot, add all ingredients, including Ground Beef Mixture..
  4. Cook on LOW for 4-5 hours (*do not cook on high...it will burn on the bottom).
  5. Serve with Crumbled Bacon and Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese (optional) or over your favorite pasta..
