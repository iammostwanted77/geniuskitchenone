Ingredients of Chocolate chip banana bread

  1. Prepare 2 cup of all-purpose flour.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of sugar.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of baking powder.
  4. It's 1/2 tsp of baking soda.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of mashed bananas.
  6. It's 1/2 cup of butter.
  7. Prepare 2 of eggs.
  8. Prepare 1 cup of semi-sweet chocolate chips.
  9. It's 1/2 cup of sour cream.
  10. It's 1 tsp of vanilla.

Chocolate chip banana bread instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F..
  2. Butter a loaf pan. Place parchment paper at the bottom so you can remove the loaf easily..
  3. Cream the butter. Then add sugar until they are smoothly combined..
  4. Add eggs and mix well with a standing mixer or a hand mixer..
  5. Add vanilla..
  6. In a separate bowl, sift the flour, baking soda and baking powder. Add this to the butter mixture..
  7. Add bananas and sour cream. Lastly, add chocolate chips..
  8. Put mixture in baking pan and bake for 1 hour and 10 minutes..
  9. Take out of the oven and poke some holes with a knife so it can cool from the center. Pass the back of the knife all around the edges so it is not stick to the pan..
  10. Let it sit for about 15 minutes, then remove from the pan and let it cool until it is at room temperature..
