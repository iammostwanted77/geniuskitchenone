Easy peanut butter cookies instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°..
  2. Mix peanut butter and sugar..
  3. Add the egg..
  4. Stir until all ingredients are well blended..
  5. Make tablespoon sized balls of cookie dough and place them on a cookie sheet..
  6. Use a fork dipped in sugar to press a crisscross pattern on the cookie dough balls..
  7. Optional: You can sprinkle a little extra sugar on top if needed..
  8. Bake for 15 mins then allow to cool down completely before taking the cookies off of the sheet..
