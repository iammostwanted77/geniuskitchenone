Ingredients of Peach sparkling water

  1. Prepare 2 of peaches.
  2. It's 1 Tbsp of (or more) lemon juice.
  3. You need 1 Tbsp of sugar, plus more to taste.
  4. It's 1 cup of water.
  5. It's 1 cup of sparkling water.
  6. Prepare cubes of Ice.
  7. It's of Mint (optional, for decoration).
  8. It's of Rosemary (optional, for decoration).

Peach sparkling water instructions

  1. Peel the peaches and save the peach skin.
  2. Cut the peaches into small cubes.
  3. Combine the peach pulp and skin in a bowl, add lemon juice and sugar.
  4. Add water to saucepan, stir in peach mixtures, heat over medium.
  5. Gently boil for 10 minutes and discard the peach skin.
  6. Cover and refrigerate the peach pulp and juice for at least 1 hour.
  7. In a glass, add ice, peach juice, peach pulp and sparkling water.
  8. Decorate with mint or rosemary.
