Ingredients of Turkey Meatloaf

  1. It's 2 lbs of LEAN ground turkey.
  2. It's 2 of eggs lightly beaten.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of finely chopped vegetables (carrots, broccoli,...).
  4. You need 1-2 cloves of fresh garlic pressed or minced.
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup of rolled oats (NOT INSTANT).
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup of milk or ketchup.
  7. It's 1 Tbsp of Tbls cumin.
  8. Prepare 1 Tbsp of Tbls garlic powder.
  9. Prepare 2 tsp of turmeric.
  10. You need 2 tsp of or eyeball, ground black pepper.
  11. You need 2 tsp of onion powder (add more if needed).
  12. You need sprinkle of cayenne pepper.
  13. Prepare pinch of salt.
