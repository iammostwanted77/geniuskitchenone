Ingredients of Taco Soup

  1. You need 2 lbs of hamburger meat.
  2. You need 2 cans (10 oz) of rotel (I used 1 hot 1 original).
  3. It's 2 cans (10 oz) of pinto beans.
  4. Prepare 2 cans (10 oz) of minestrone soup.
  5. Prepare of Small block of Velvetta cheese (I used 16oz/1lb).
  6. It's 1 pkg of taco seasoning.
