Ingredients of Green Bean Casserole

  1. You need 2 packages (10 oz) of frozen green beans.
  2. You need 4 slice of bacon diced.
  3. Prepare 2/3 cup of onion chopped small.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of all purpose flour.
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of sugar.
  6. You need 1/2 cup of milk.
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup of mayo.
  8. You need 1 dash of salt and pepper.
  9. It's 1/2 cup of chedder cheese shredded.
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup of buttered Ritz cracker crumbs.
  11. It's 1/2 cup of french fried onions.

Green Bean Casserole step by step

  1. Pre heat oven to 350°F.
  2. Cook green beans according to package directions then drain.
  3. Cook bacon in a large skillet untill crisp.
  4. Remove bacon drain and set aside and save drippings.
  5. Saute onion in bacon drippings untill tender remove onion and set aside.
  6. Add flour and sugar to bacon drippings cook for 1 minute stirring well.
  7. Add milk, salt and pepper and cook stirring constantly until thickened.
  8. Stir in mayo, green beans, bacon, and onions.
  9. Spoon into a lightly greased 1 1/2 quart casserole dish.
  10. Sprinkle with cheese, fried onions, and cracker crumbs.
  11. Bake at 350 degreese for 20 minutes.
