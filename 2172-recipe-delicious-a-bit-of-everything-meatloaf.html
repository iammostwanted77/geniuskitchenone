A bit of everything meatloaf step by step

  1. Boil the 6 large eggs. Peel and set aside. crisp the bacon and crumble it up.
  2. Preheat oven 350° Fahrenheit.
  3. Grate the carrot, chop the onion, finely chop the yellow pepper, chop the spinach more, and crumble the bacon. Set in a mixing bowl..
  4. To the bowl add the meats, 2 raw egg, the olives, salt, cumin, and garlic mix all this together well..
  5. Spred it out flat. Add boiled eggs all the way across it..
  6. Now cover it up keeping the boiled eggs in center..

  10. Crush pistachios and sprinkle on top. Surround with cauliflower. Bake till done about 90 minutes. Let sit 7 minutes..
