Ingredients of Perfect Peanut Butter Cookies

  1. Prepare 1 cup of Sugar.
  2. It's 1 cup of Light Brown Sugar, Packed.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of Creamy Peanut Butter.
  4. It's 1/2 cup of Vegetable Shrtening.
  5. It's 2 of Eggs.
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of Flour.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Baking Soda.
  8. It's 1 cup of Peanut Butter Chips.
  9. You need 2/3 cup of Toffee chips.

Perfect Peanut Butter Cookies instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350. Prepare and area for cookies to cool..
  2. Combine the sugars, peanut butter, and shortening in a large bowl. Beat with an electric beater until smooth, and then beat in the eggs. Set aside..
  3. Combine flour and baking soda and gradually add to the creamed mixture. At the point I usually mix by hand, but you can use a low speed with your electric mixer if you desire..
  4. Once that is blended I stir in the peanut butter chips and crumbled toffee..
  5. Drop by the rounded spoonfuls on cookie sheet. Place them two inches apart on un-greased cookie sheets. Use a fork to slightly flatten the cookie and to give it that classic criss-cross pattern..
  6. Bake for 9 to 12 minutes or until the bottoms are lightly browned and set. Don't over bake these cookies. The gooeyer the better!.
  7. Allow the cookies to completely cool and enjoy!.
