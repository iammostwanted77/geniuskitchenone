Recipe: Yummy Beetroot Quinoa

Delicious, fresh and tasty.

Beetroot Quinoa.

You can have Beetroot Quinoa using 11 ingredients and 9 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Beetroot Quinoa

  1. You need 1 cup of quinoa, boiled.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of beetroot, grated.
  3. You need 1/4 cup of carrot, grated.
  4. It's 1/2 cup of peas, frozen.
  5. Prepare 50 gm of paneer, cubed.
  6. Prepare 2 of garlic clove, chopped.
  7. It's 1/4 tsp of ghee.
  8. It's 1/4 tsp of red chilli powder.
  9. You need 1/2 tsp of cumin powder.
  10. Prepare 1/8 tsp of black pepper powder.
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp of salt or to taste.

Beetroot Quinoa instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan. Add garlic and saute it for a min or till it turns golden..
  2. Now add beetroot and saute it on high flame for a min..
  3. Next add carrot and peas and mix well..
  4. Next add red chilli powder, cumin powder, black pepper & salt..
  5. Saute this on high flame for min..
  6. Add cooked quinoa and saute on high flame for 40 secs..
  7. Turn flame to lowest and cover with lid for a min..
  8. Turn off the flame and toss in the chopped paneer cubes into the dish..
  9. Serve hot with lauki ka raita/ dahi/Chutney..
