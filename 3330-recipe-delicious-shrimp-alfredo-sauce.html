Ingredients of Shrimp Alfredo sauce

  1. You need 1 pound of cooked small shrimp.
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 c of milk.
  3. You need 1/2 c of grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. You need 1 Tbsp of butter.
  5. You need 1 Tbsp of flour.
  6. Prepare of Salt.
  7. Prepare of Pepper.
  8. It's 1 of small pinch crushed red pepper (opinional).

Shrimp Alfredo sauce step by step

  1. In sauce pan melt butter on medium low heat then whisk in flour.
  2. Then add the milk then let boil 2-5 mins.
  3. Then add cheese then simmer and reduce the heat to low.
  4. Then add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Chopped the shrimp and remove the tails.
  6. Then add to the sauce cook it for a couple of seconds to heat the shrimp.
  7. You can add anything type of pasta.
  8. Then serve and enjoy ;).
  9. You can use chicken or steak..
