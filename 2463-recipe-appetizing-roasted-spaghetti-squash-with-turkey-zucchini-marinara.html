Roasted Spaghetti Squash with Turkey Zucchini Marinara step by step

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400.
  2. Halve spaghetti squash, brush with olive oil, a generous pinch of salt and pepper, and place on lined baking sheet.
  3. Roast for 50-60 minutes until fork easily pierces flesh.
  4. Meanwhile, brown turkey in a hot pan with 1 tbsp olive oil..
  5. Reserve cooked turkey and saute red onion. When soft, add zucchini and cook for 7-10 minutes.
  6. Add back in turkey, can of tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, and spices (chili flakes, oregano, thyme). Bring to a boil, and simmer for 20-30 minutes while Squash finishes roasting.
  7. When squash is finished, shred with fork, top with sauce, and enjoy!.
