Ingredients of Football Chilli

  1. Prepare 4.5 lbs of groundbeef.
  2. Prepare 2 tablespoons of Seasoning of choice(Dale's seasonings).
  3. Prepare 1 can of petite diced tomatoes.
  4. Prepare 1 can of tomato sauce.
  5. Prepare 1 can of roasted corn.
  6. You need 2 of large can of tomatoe juice.
  7. You need 6 packets of Chilli powder.
  8. Prepare 1 can of black beans.
  9. Prepare 1 can of chilli beans.
  10. It's 1 can of kidney beans.
  11. You need 1 of small onion diced petite and sauteed.
  12. You need of Toppings to add to the chilli.
  13. You need of Shredded Cheese.
  14. It's of Jalapeno peppers.
  15. Prepare of Hot sauce.
  16. You need of Sour cream.
  17. You need of Spaghetti.
  18. It's 1 batch of cornbread.
  19. Prepare of Petite diced onions.
  20. It's of Tortilla chips.
  21. It's of Oyster Crackers.

Football Chilli instructions

  1. In skillet fry the ground beef.(I use Dale's seasoning or you can use my recipe I make when I run out of Dale's) Season with my substitute version of Dale's, or dale's seasoning picture below. In a real pinch use Worcestershire, steak seasoning (I use longhorn)..
  2. Saute the onions in butter in the same pan that you fried the groundbeef in til desired doneiness..
  3. In large pot add the juice, sauce, beans, chilli powder, petite tomatoes, corn, cooked groundbeef and the sauteed onions. Let simmer for 1/2 hr..
  4. Ladle up a bowl of chilli. Top with toppings of choice (I like cornbread, cheese, sour cream and hot sauce). Enjoy..
