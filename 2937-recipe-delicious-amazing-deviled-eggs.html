Amazing Deviled Eggs step by step

  2. cut horizontally and remove yolk into a bowl.
  3. add all ingredients into the bowl with the yolks, except for the paprika and mix together.
  4. fill the egg with the mixture of ingredients and sprinkle the top with paprika.
  5. *I used 3 eggs, but you can use however many you want and adjust the amount of ingredients accordingly. all amounts were estimates and not actually measured out..
