Beef Qeema naan step by step

  1. For dough, mix all the dry ingredients and then make dough with Lukewarm milk..
  2. Add all the qeema filling ingredients in qeema. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan and fry qeema until it's slightly cooked..
  3. Make pairas with the dough. Roti ki Jesey bail lay..
  4. Put filling in the middle pick up dough from the sides twist it and gently press kar dey. Paratha ki tarah bail lay. Parathey ki nisbet thora mota rakhey..
  5. Any twa or nonstick pan main parathey ki jesey pakain. First one side pakney dey,then oil daal kar turn kar lay aur pakain..
  6. Enjoy qeema naan with any chutney or with any thing you like..
