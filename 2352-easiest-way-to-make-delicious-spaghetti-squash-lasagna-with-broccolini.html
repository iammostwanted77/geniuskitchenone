Spaghetti Squash Lasagna with Broccolini step by step

  1. Position oven racks in upper and lower thirds; preheat oven to 450°F..
  2. Cook squash using either microwave or oven method. Cut in half lengthwise..
  3. In large skillet, heat oil. Add broccolini, garlic and red pepper. Cook, stirring frequently, for 2 min. Add water and cook, stirring until broccolini is tender. Transfer to large bowl..
  4. Use a fork to scrap the squash from the shells and add to broccolini mixture. Place squash shells in baking dish..
  5. Add in 3/4 cup cheese, 2 T Parmesan, Italian seasoning, salt and pepper. Mix well..
  6. Divide mixture between the two shells. Top with remaing 1/4 cup cheese and 2 T Parmesan..
  7. Bake on lower rack for 10 min. Move to upper rack, turn broiler to high and broil until cheese starts to brown, about 2 min..
