Italian Turkey Meatloaf instructions

  1. Chop onion and mushrooms and saut√© in a little olive oil.
  2. Mix bread crumbs, eggs, 1/3 cup ketchup, milk, sugar, garlic, pepper, salt and Italian seasoning in a bowl....
  3. Add ground meat, onion and mushrooms to breadcrumb mixture and mix well.
  4. Preheat oven to 350.
  5. Mix 1/2 cup ketchup, BBQ sauce, honey, hot sauce and brown sugar in a small mixing bowl.
  6. Line a baking pan with aluminum foil, put meat mixture in pan and form in a football shape.
  7. Add half of the ketchup mixture to top of meatloaf spreading it evenly.
  8. Pour water in pan around the meatloaf.
  9. Bake for for 40 minutes, then add remaining ketchup mixture, then bake for another 20 minutes..
