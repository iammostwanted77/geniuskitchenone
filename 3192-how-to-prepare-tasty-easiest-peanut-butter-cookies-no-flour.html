Easiest Peanut Butter cookies!! NO FLOUR!!:-) step by step

  1. Preheat oven 375.
  2. Mix all ingredients by hand in bowl. No, you do not use flour for these cookies :-).
  3. Cover cookie sheet with foil for easier clean-up and removal of cookies. Spray light coat of cooking spray on foil..
  4. Either drop teaspoon full drops on foil, or I gently take small amount in hand and roll (gently) and place on sheet..
  5. Spray a fork with spray or you can sprinkle sugar on rounds and using fork press cookies down in criss/cross pattern..
  6. Bake for 12-15 min depending on size of cookie. Cookies are FRAGILE so very gently remove from sheet and let cool..
