Egg Yolk Peanut Butter Cookies instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F..
  2. Cream together margarine, 1/4 cup PB, and two sugars. (Use a fork).
  3. Add egg yolks, 1 cup PB, and vanilla. Mix well..
  4. Sift in flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until just incorporated. (Will be crumbly).
  5. Form into patties and place 2 cm apart on a parchment lined tray; they don't spread much. Press a cross hatch pattern into the cookies carefully, or they'll crumble..
  6. Bake for 15 minutes..
