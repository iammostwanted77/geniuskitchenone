  2. It's 1 tsp of sea salt.

  4. Prepare 1 1/2 cups of water.
  5. It's of Onion.
  6. You need of Tuna.
  7. It's of Cilantro.
  8. Prepare of Feta Cheese.

Quinoa w/ Feta cheese instructions

  1. Place the Quinoa with the water, salt, and consomé and heat it up and stir it until it starts boiling. You have to stir it a lot so the water actually wets the quinoa fully.
  2. Once it starts bubbling and boiling you stir it, cover it with a lid, lower the heat to very low heat and leave it..
  3. Keep checking it until all the water has been absorbed or condensed..
  4. Then you can stick a chopstick or fork to the bottom and check all the water is gone, you turn off the heat, move it off from the heat, stir it a bit, and let it cool off a bit..
  5. After it's cooled off a bit, add the feta cheese, onion, tuna, tomato, and cilantro and mix well..
