Delicious Meatloaf Dinner instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F, and lightly greasea cookie sheet..
  2. Make sauce: add brown sugar, ketchup, ginger, worstechire sauce and mix well. Set aside..
  3. In another bowl: add remaining ingredients: beef,milk,egg,salt, pepper,onion, garlic, celery, bread crumbs. Mix with hands and drop over mixture kn lightly greased cokkie sheet. Mold into a loaf . place sauce mixture over the loaf (covering it all) and place in oven for 1hr (or until juices clear)..
  4. Serve with mashed potatoes and asparagus. Enjoy.
