Instant & healthy Breakfast Ragi Dosa instructions

  1. Take a bowl add ragi flour, rice flour, chopped curry leaves, chopped green chillies, salt, curd and water mix well. Make watery type batter..
  2. Turn on gas, placed dosa pan, greased with oil and put ragi batter on it. Add some oil over it. Turn after 2 minutes cook another side..
  3. Eat as it is or with chutney..
