Deviled eggs tray step by step

  1. Boil eggs for 10 minutes with dash of baking soda and salt for easier peeling. Cool..
  2. Peel eggs. Cut eggs in half and put yolks into Bowl. Mash yolks with fork. Add the mayonnaise Worcestershire sauce, celery and Mrs. Dash. Whip with beaters until creamy..
  3. With egg House entry add a scoop of yolk mixture. Sprinkle with paprika and then parsley..
