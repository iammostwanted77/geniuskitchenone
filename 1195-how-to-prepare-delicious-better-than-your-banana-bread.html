Ingredients of Better Than Your Banana Bread

  1. Prepare 2 cup of flour.
  2. It's 1 cup of sugar.
  3. Prepare 1 tsp of cinnamon.
  4. It's 1 tsp of nutmeg.
  5. It's 1 tsp of baking pouder.
  6. It's 1 tsp of baking soda.
  7. You need 2 large of eggs.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of butter.
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp of vanilla extract.
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup of olive oil.
  11. It's 1/2 cup of honey.
  12. It's 1/2 cup of applesauce.
  13. It's 3 of bananas.
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup of your desired crushed nuts *optional*.

Better Than Your Banana Bread instructions

  1. preheat oven to 325*.
  2. mix dry ingredents in bowl.
  3. mash up very finely 3 bananas in another bowl and mix in the applesauce.
  4. beat eggs and mix in the rest of the wet ingredients in another bowl.
  5. mix dry ingredients into the wet ingredients at a medium speed.
  6. mix in bananas.
  7. mix in your desired nuts *optional *.
  8. pour into pan, may need to be split into two loaf pans.
  9. cook for 1 hr 10 mins. or until done, turn heat up to 350 for the last 10 mins. for a more golden brown top.
