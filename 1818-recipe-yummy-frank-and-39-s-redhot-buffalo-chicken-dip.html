Ingredients of Frank's redhot buffalo chicken dip

  1. You need 8 oz of package of cream cheese.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of blue cheese salad dressing.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of Frank's redhot sauce.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of crumbled blue cheese or shredded mozzarella cheese.
  5. Prepare 2 cup of shredded cooked chicken.

Frank's redhot buffalo chicken dip step by step

  1. Heat oven to 350°F. Place cream cheese into deep dis 9 inch pie pan. Miccrowave 1 minute to soften..
  2. Whisk in salad dressing, frank's sauce, and cheese until smooth. Stir in Chicken..
  3. Bake for 20 minutes or until mixture is heated through; stir. Garnish as desired. Serve with crackers or vegetables..
