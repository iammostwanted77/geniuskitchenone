How to Make Tasty Paprika Chili Stew

Delicious, fresh and tasty.

Paprika Chili Stew.

You can have Paprika Chili Stew using 18 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Paprika Chili Stew

  1. Prepare of Stew------------------.
  2. It's 30 ounces of canned pinto beans.
  3. Prepare 1/2 pound of baby carrots.
  4. It's 15 ounce of can of black beans.
  5. Prepare 1-1/2 pound of ground beef.
  6. It's 1-1/2 pound of carrots.
  7. Prepare 2 of ribs of celery.
  8. It's 29 ounces of tomato puree.
  9. You need 2 of medium onion.
  10. You need of Spices-----------------.
  11. You need 1/3 cup of ground paprika.
  12. You need 2 tablespoons of smoked paprika.
  13. Prepare 2 teaspoon of ground Pink Himalayan salt.
  14. It's 1 tablespoon of granulated garlic powder.
  15. Prepare 1 teaspoon of ground ginger.
  16. Prepare 2 tablespoons of liquid smoke.
  17. Prepare 1/4 cup of chopped parsley flakes.
  18. It's 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce.

Paprika Chili Stew step by step

  1. Wash your vegetables. Chop the peel and carrots, celery, and onions..
  2. Add the ground beef to a pot. Add the spices except the liquid smoke..
  3. Add the chopped vegetables, and baby carrots. Stir well and cover..
  4. Brown the beef with the vegetables stirring occasionally.
  5. Cook till the juices the beef and Vegetables produced are absorbed or evaporated. Careful not to burn the dish..
  6. Add the beans and tomato puree. Also this is where you add the liquid smoke and Worcestershire sauce.
  7. Simmering and gently stirring occasionally, the beans can lose their integrity, for 25-30 minutes covered. Keep it going till carrots are fork tender and carefully paying attention to it possibly sticking to the bottom of pot..
  8. Now we have finished it time to eat! I hope you enjoy!!.
