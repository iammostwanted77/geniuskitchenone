Ingredients of Bacon & Pumpkin Spaghetti

  1. It's 1/2 of pumpkin or butternut squash.
  2. You need of Bacon (as much as you want - LOL).
  3. You need 1 of onion, diced really small.
  4. Prepare of cooked spaghetti.
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup of pecorino or your favorite pasta cheese mix.
  6. You need 1 dash of salt.
  7. It's 1 dash of pepper.
  8. It's 1/2 tsp of olive oil.
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of chicken powder.

Bacon & Pumpkin Spaghetti instructions

  1. Toss in bacon, onion and pumpkin cubes into an oiled tray and bake in oven at approximately 150 degrees for 35 minutes (depends on your oven). Toss every now and then.
  2. Cook spaghetti over boiling water for 11 to 12 minutes, drain. Sprinkle in a little bit of salt, pepper, olive oil and chicken powder and mix.
  3. When bacon and pumpkin is done, mix it in with the spaghetti and chow down!.
