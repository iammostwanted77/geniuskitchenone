Yellow rice W/Red Beans & Surprise Meatloaf step by step

  1. Heat the oil in a pot over medium heat..
  2. Add the sofrito and olives. Lightly fry for about one minute..
  3. Add the sazón, bouillon and beans. Stir, when sauce start a boiling point, add the rice..
  4. Pour in the water and bring to a rolling boil. Allow to boil, and then stir the rice mixture..
  5. Reduce the heat to mid-low and cover tight fit lid. Don't lift the lid during the cooking time..
  6. Cook for 25 minutes. Remove the lid, fluff the rice, and then cover again and let sit a few minutes before serving in low or simmer temp..
