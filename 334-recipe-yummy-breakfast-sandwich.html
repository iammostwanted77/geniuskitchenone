Ingredients of Breakfast sandwich

  1. Prepare 1/2 of onion.
  2. Prepare 2 of bagels.
  3. Prepare 1 handful of spinach, roughly chopped.
  4. You need 2 tsp of Mustard.
  5. You need 3 slices of American cheese.
  6. It's 4 of breakfast sausages, casings removed.
  7. You need 1 tsp of vegetable oil.

Breakfast sandwich instructions

  1. Slice the onion, fry it in the oil. Scoop in a slotted spoon and set aside once golden on a paper towel..
  2. Mold the sausage meat into two bagel-shaped forms. Pan fry in the onion oil..
  3. Toast the bagels lightly.
  4. Add mustard and spinach to one half of the bagel, and mustard and onions to the other half. Top both with a thin layer of cheese. Broil until cheese is melted..
  5. Add sausage ring, squish sandwich together, and enjoy!.
