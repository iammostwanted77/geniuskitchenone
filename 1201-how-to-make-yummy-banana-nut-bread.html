You can have Banana Nut Bread using 18 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Banana Nut Bread

  1. It's of Bread.
  2. It's 1-1/2 cups of almond flour.
  3. You need 1 cup of coconut flour.
  4. It's 1 tablespoon of peanut butter.
  5. It's 1/4 cup of sour cream.
  6. You need 3 of ripe bananas.
  7. You need 1 teaspoons of vanilla extract.
  8. It's 3 of large eggs.
  9. It's 1 teaspoons of baking soda.
  10. Prepare 2/3 cup of honey.
  11. Prepare of Whole Nuts.
  12. It's 1/2 cup of walnuts.
  13. Prepare 1 cup of cashew nuts.
  14. It's of Spices.
  15. It's 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon.
  16. It's 1/2 teaspoon of ground nutmeg.
  17. Prepare 1/3 teaspoon of ground cloves.
  18. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of kosher salt.

Banana Nut Bread instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit..
  2. Mix the bread ingredients together. Mash the bananas well. Make sure everything is incorporated well..
  3. Add the nuts and spices..
  4. Add to a greased loaf pan and bake for forty minutes to an hour till its firm and done. Add butter pats on top..
  5. Serve with butter I hope you enjoy!!.
