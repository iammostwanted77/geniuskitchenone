Ingredients of Meatloaf (my way)

  1. It's 1 1/2 lb of Ground beef.
  2. It's 1/2 lb of mild ground Sausage.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of Italian breadcrumbs.
  4. It's 1 of Eggs.
  5. It's 1 of Onion, minced.
  6. Prepare 1/2 of Green pepper, minced.
  7. It's 1 tsp of each, Sage, Pepper, Salt, Parsley.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of Ketchup.
  9. It's 1 of Additional ketchup for topping.

Meatloaf (my way) step by step

  1. Mix all ingredients in large bowl..
  2. Make into loaf and place in large baking dish..
  3. Add ketchup on top as glaze..
  4. Bake 350° for 1 hr..
  5. Remove from oven and prop up pan so fat drains away. Let stand 10 min..
  6. Slice and serve..
