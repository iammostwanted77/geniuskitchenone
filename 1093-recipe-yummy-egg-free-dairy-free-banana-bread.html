Ingredients of Egg-free, Dairy-free Banana Bread

  1. Prepare 4 of bananas.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of unflavored oil (Carnola oil, Sunflower seed oil, ect).
  3. It's 300 ml of soy milk.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of sugar.
  5. Prepare 3 cup of all-purpose flour.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of baking powder.
  7. It's 1 cup of chopped walnuts (or your favourite nuts).
  8. Prepare of dried fruit (optional, I used slice apple).

Egg-free, Dairy-free Banana Bread step by step

  1. Mash 3 bananas and slice 1 banana.
  2. Mix mashed banana with oil, soy milk and sugar.
  3. Sieve in the flour and baking powder. Stir everything together with rubber spatula..
  4. Add sliced banana, nuts and dried frite..
  5. Pour the batter in loaf pan lined with baking paper or aluminium foil cup. Bake at 180°C for 30-45 minutes..
  6. Keep the banana bread 24hr before serve for better taste..
