Splenda peanut butter cookies instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375°.
  2. Mix Splenda, brown sugar Splenda, and butter..
  3. add peanut butter, milk, vanilla, and egg. mix.
  4. stir in flour, baking soda, and salt. mix.
  5. shape into balls and do a criss cross pattern on the top of cookies.
  6. bake until desired brownness is achieved.
