  3. It's 2 large of eggs.

  7. It's 1/4 of nuts.

Delicious banana bread step by step

  1. Grease a tray with butter and sprinkle it with flour or breadcrumbs..
  2. Mash the bananas very well with a fork or a blender.
  3. In a bowl, mix well sugar and butter at the room temperature until soft and smooth..
  4. Than add eggs and mix again.
  5. Add the bananas over sugar and butter and mix.
  6. Add the flour and baking powder . But not all at once.And mix well.
  7. Than chopped nuts.
  8. Pour batter into cake pan, be sure thetray is a bit higher cause the cake is going to swollen inside the oven.
  9. Leave it in the oven about 1 hour a until well browned. Then remove and leave for 10 minutes in the pan to cool..
  10. After 10 minutes turn it upside down on a plate and leave to cool completely..
