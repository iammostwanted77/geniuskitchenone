Ingredients of No Bake Peanut Butter Cookies

  1. You need 1 stick of butter.
  2. You need 2 cup of granulated sugar.
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup of milk.
  4. It's 3/4 cup of peanut butter.
  5. You need 3 1/2 cup of old fashioned oats.
  6. You need 2 tsp of vanilla extract.
