Mango custard trifle instructions

  2. Make vanilla custard as per packet instructions..
  3. Make mango puree by blending mango cubes, sugar and 2 tblsp of water..
  4. Layer trifle by first adding vanilla custard, jelly, biscuits, mango puree, fresh fruits and repeat..
  5. Garnish with fruits and nuts..
