Ingredients of colored deviled eggs

  1. You need 1 dozen of eggs.
  2. You need 1 box of food color or easter egg dye.
  3. You need 1 tsp of Vinegar per color.
  4. Prepare 1 cup of water per color.
  5. It's 1/2 cup of mayonnaise.
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp of mustard.
  7. Prepare 1 pinch of paprika.

colored deviled eggs instructions

  1. boil eggs for about 15 minutes.
  2. run cold water into hot pot of eggs for a few mintes then put ice in water and let set around 5 minutes.
  3. add water, vinegar, and color together.
  4. cut eggs open remove egg yolks into a bowl. set egg yolks aside.
  5. rinse any excess yolk off the eggs then places eggs into food color mix leave in until desired color is reached.
  6. mix egg yolks, mustard, mayonnaise, and paprika.
  7. fill eggs with yolk mix.
  8. sprinkle with paprika.
