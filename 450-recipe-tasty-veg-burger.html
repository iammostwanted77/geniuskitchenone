Veg Burger instructions

  1. Take a bowl add boiled potato little salt pepper powder make patties..
  2. Heat 1-2 tbsp. oil in a pan and shallow fry the patties on a low flame on both sides till well browned. Drain on a kitchen towel and keep aside..
  3. To assemble the burger - Spread cheese slice on one side of the burger and mayonnaise on the other side of the burger Sprinkle some pepper powder..
  4. Place a lettuce leaf followed by the patty, onion, tomato, cucumber, Finally close with the bun and serve with strawberry shake..
