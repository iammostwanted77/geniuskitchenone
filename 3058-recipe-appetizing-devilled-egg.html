Devilled Egg instructions

  1. First boil the eggs then separate yolk from inside..
  2. Keep egg white in food colour water for 10 min.
  3. Mix boiled egg yolk with mayonnaise and add black pepper and salt to make a paste..
  4. Fill this paste in cone shape plastic bag..
  5. Drain water and separate egg white which is now colored..
  6. Fill egg yolk paste in the mid of egg yolk and garnish it.it's ready to eat..
