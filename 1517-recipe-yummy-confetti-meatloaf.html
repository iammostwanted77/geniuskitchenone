Confetti Meatloaf.

You can cook Confetti Meatloaf using 11 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Confetti Meatloaf

  1. It's 1.5 of ground beef.
  2. You need 2 of carrots shredded.
  3. Prepare 1/2 of onion shredded.
  4. You need 1/3 cup of plain oats.
  5. You need 2 teaspoons of salt.
  6. You need Teaspoon of garlic powder.
  7. Prepare 1 cup of Annie's organic ketchup.
  8. It's Dash of Worcester shire sauce.
  9. It's 1/2 cup of brown sugar.
  10. You need 1 tablespoon of lemon juice.
  11. It's 3 teaspoons of ground mustard.

Confetti Meatloaf instructions

  1. Dice vegetables, then shred them in a food processor..
  2. Add oatmeal, salt, and garlic powder to the meat and mix thoroughly. I'd advise getting your hands dirty, instead of using a wooden spoon..
  3. Add shredded vegetables and mix them in.
  4. Spray coconut oil on your bread pan. Place into pan and cook at 350 for 1 hour.
  5. Mix ketchup, lemon juice, Worcester shire sauce, brown sugar, & ground mustard. Then cook on medium heat for 3 minutes..
  6. Drizzle sauce on top or put to the side & enjoy..
