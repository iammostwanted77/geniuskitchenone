Ingredients of Gyoza dumplings

  1. You need 200 grams of ground pork.
  2. It's 1/6 of (of the whole) of finely chopped cabbages.
  3. You need 2 handfuls of finely chopped green onions.
  4. You need 1 clove of garlic grated.
  5. You need 10 grams of ginger grated.
  6. You need 2 tablespoons of soy sauce.
  7. It's 2 tablespoons of sake or wine.
  8. It's 1 tablespoon of sesame oil.
  9. Prepare 2 teaspoons of starch(any starch).
  10. Prepare of Some salt.
  11. Prepare of Gyoza wraps(pretty much pizza dough without yeast).
