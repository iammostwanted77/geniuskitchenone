Amazing meatloaf step by step

  1. Thaw ground beef.
  2. Crack egg into large bowl dump in ground beef.
  3. Add stuffing mix to bowl with 1cup water.
  4. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  5. Smush mixture in bowl together until mixed very well.
  6. Put into oven for 50 minutes.
  7. Pour ketchup on top of meatloaf return to oven for 10 minutes.
  8. Take out of oven let cool briefly serve and eat.
