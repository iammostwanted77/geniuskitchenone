Junes frickin awesome deviled eggs! step by step

  1. Boil eggs for about 20 min. Take shell off eggs and cut in half..
  2. Take yoke out of eggs put in a bowl. Mix the yoke up so its fine add salt..
  3. Add mayonnaise, mustard, ranch and relish juice. Then mix in onion powder and pepper..
  4. Mix well place even amounts of yoke into each egg..
  5. Add paprika if desired as garnish. Let cool..
