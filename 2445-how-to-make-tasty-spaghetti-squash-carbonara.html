Ingredients of Spaghetti Squash Carbonara

  1. It's 1 1/4 tsp of Fresh cracked pepper.
  2. You need 1 of large Spaghetti squash.
  3. It's 4 slice of Bacon - cut into small strips crosswise.
  4. It's 2 tsp of Minced garlic.
  5. It's 1/4 cup of Chicken broth.
  6. You need 2 of Egg yolks.
  7. Prepare 1 of Egg.
  8. Prepare 1 cup of Grated Parmesan cheese.

Spaghetti Squash Carbonara instructions

  1. Preheat to 375°F.
  2. Cook spaghetti squash 1 hour.
  3. Cook bacon.
  4. Add garlic - sauté one minute.
  5. Add broth and cook until liquid has evaporated.
  6. In medium bowl whisk eggs with cheese.
  7. Season with pepper.
  8. Combine eggs with bacon mixture, warming the eggs in the pan, don't cook the eggs.
  9. Add to spaghetti squash and toss.
