Red Quinoa upma instructions

  1. After washing both types of quinoa 3-4 times under running water, cook it in an open pan in water using very little salt.
  2. Heat oil in a non-stick pan and add mustard seeds, when they crackle add curry leaves..
  3. Add peanuts and sauté a little.
  4. Add chopped onions and green chillies and sauté, till onions turn pink.
  5. Now sauté boiled peas,corn kernels And chopped carrots too till the raw smell goes away.
  6. Now add the seasoning according to your taste and then add cooked quinoa Mix everything well with light hands with the help of a spatula.
  7. Garnish with Coriander leaves.
