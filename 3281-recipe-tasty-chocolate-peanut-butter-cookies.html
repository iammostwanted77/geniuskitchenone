Ingredients of chocolate peanut butter cookies

  1. Prepare 2 cup of sugar.
  2. You need 1 stick of butter.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of milk.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of peanut butter.
  5. You need 1 tbsp of cocoa.
  6. You need 2 cup of oatmeal.

chocolate peanut butter cookies instructions

  1. mix cocoa , sugar, butter and milk.
  2. bring to boil for 2 min exactly.
  3. remove from heat. stir in peanut butter and oatmeal. mix well..
