Ingredients of Slow Cooker Green Bean Casserole

  1. Prepare 1 stick of Velveeta Cheese.
  2. It's 2 can of Family Size Green Beans.
  3. Prepare 10 each of Red skin potatoes, unskinned.
  4. You need 1/2 packages of Bacon, crumbled.

Slow Cooker Green Bean Casserole step by step

  1. Microwave potatoes: 10 min.
  2. Dice potatoes.
  3. Drain green beans.
  4. Add all ingredients to slow cooker. Set on high.
  5. Cook until cheese is melted and potatoes are soft.
  6. Salt and pepper to taste.
