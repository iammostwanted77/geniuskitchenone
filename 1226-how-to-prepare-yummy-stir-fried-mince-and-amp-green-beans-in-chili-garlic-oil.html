Stir-fried Mince & Green Beans in Chili Garlic Oil.

You can have Stir-fried Mince & Green Beans in Chili Garlic Oil using 11 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Stir-fried Mince & Green Beans in Chili Garlic Oil

  1. It's 1/8-1/4 of Kilo Pork Mince.
  2. Prepare 1 handful of Beans, ends removed, bite-size cut.
  3. It's 1/2 of small red onion, chopped.
  4. It's 2 cloves of garlic, chopped.
  5. It's 1-2 tsp of chilli garlic oil or sauce.
  6. You need 1 Tbsp of cooking oil.
  7. You need 1 tsp of butter.
  8. You need 1 handful of moringa leaves (optional) or substitute spinach.
  9. It's 1 dash of Knorr or Maggi seasoning or any soy seasoning.
  10. It's to taste of Salt.
  11. You need to taste of Pepper.

Stir-fried Mince & Green Beans in Chili Garlic Oil step by step

  1. Sauté in cooking oil the garlic and onions..
  2. Add the pork mince. Season with 2 pinches of salt and sprinkle pepper to taste. Sauté until colour changes. Add few drops of Knorr..
  3. Add the blanched (already half cooked) beans. Add the butter. Continue stir fying..
  4. Add the moringa leaves and the chilli garlic oil or sauce. Adjust according to how hot u want it. Mix and saute in well until beans are cooked. Serve and enjoy..
