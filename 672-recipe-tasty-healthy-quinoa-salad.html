Ingredients of Healthy quinoa salad

  1. Prepare 5 tablespoons of olive oil (for dressing).
  2. You need 2 tablespoons of Apple cider vinegar (for dressing).
  3. You need 3 cloves of garlic (for dressing).
  4. You need 0.5 of lemon juiced (for dressing).
  5. It's 2 tablespoons of 0% Greek yogurt (for dressing).
  6. It's 1 cup of quinoa (uncooked).
  7. Prepare 1 of red bell pepper.
  8. Prepare 1 of yellow bell pepper.
  9. You need 1.5 cups of halved grape tomatoes.
  10. It's .25 cup of green olives.
  11. Prepare 3 tablespoons of chopped parsley.
  12. It's 2 tablespoons of fresh dill.
  13. You need to taste of Salt and pepper.
  14. You need of Top with pumpkin seeds (optional).

Healthy quinoa salad instructions

  1. Measure out 1cup of quinoa and follow the cooking instruction on the package..
  2. Combine olive oil, vinegar, garlic, lemon juice, and Greek yogurt in a medium sized bowl and whisk until combined. Salt and pepper to taste..
  3. Once the quinoa has finished cooking, allow to cool completely before proceeding..
  4. Dice both bell peppers, halve the tomatoes and remove the pits from the olives if necessary. You may also roughly chop the parsley and dill at this time..
  5. Once the quinoa has cooled, transfer it to a large mixing bowl. Add the herbs, vegetables and dressing and stir the salad until all of the ingredients combined..
  6. Be sure to taste the salad before serving to be sure it is seasoned to your liking. If you are satisfied both the taste, you may either top it with the pumpkin seeds and serve it immediately or you may store it and eat it later. Enjoy!.
