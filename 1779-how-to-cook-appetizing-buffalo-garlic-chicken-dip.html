buffalo garlic chicken dip step by step

  1. preheat oven to 400° farenheit.
  2. drain water from cans of chicken.
  3. melt together cream cheese, ranch, buffalo sauce, and minced garlic on stove at medium heat.
  4. mix in cans of chicken and stir until chi ken breaks up into smaller pieces.
  5. remove from stove and pour into baking pan.
  6. sprinkle cheddar cheese on top.
  7. put in oven at 400° F for 10-15 minutes or until cheese has melted.
