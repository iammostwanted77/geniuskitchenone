Ingredients of Chilli con Carne

  1. You need 500 g of minced beef.
  2. It's 2 of small onions.
  3. You need 2 of gloves of garlic.
  4. It's 70 g of tomato paste.
  5. It's 2 tsp of spices.
  6. You need 400 g of kidneybeans.
  7. Prepare 400 g of canned tomatoes (diced).
  8. Prepare 140 g of canned pineapple.

Chilli con Carne step by step

  1. Finely dice the onions and mince the garlic..
  2. Fruit the onion and garlic in a bit of olive oil on low heat, untill the onions turn soft..
  3. Add the spices and tomato paste, and cook for just a minute to take of the raw edge..
  4. Add the minced meat and work on medium heat it till it has your desired structure and browning. For me that is fine and dark brown..
  5. Add the canned tomatoes, the beans and the pineapple. And let simmer uncovered for at least 20 minutes or untill it has your favorite consistency..
  6. Season to taste, and serve with boiled potatoes, rice, bread or add some more beans and eat it as is. Optional add some shredded cheese..
