Ingredients of Very easy banana bread

  1. It's 250 grams of Plain flour.
  2. Prepare 1 tsp of Bicarbonate of soda.
  3. It's 1 pinch of Salt.
  4. You need 115 grams of Fine Brown sugar.
  5. Prepare 115 grams of butter.
  6. Prepare 2 medium of Eggs beaten.
  7. Prepare 500 grams of Over ripe banana's.

Very easy banana bread instructions

  2. Grease and line a 23 x 13 loaf tin.
  3. Combine flour and bicarbonate of soda and salt in a separate bowl..
  4. In another bowl cream sugar and butter together and add eggs..
  5. Mash banana' s and add to your wet mixture combine well..
  6. Add the floor mixture to the wet mix and stir in easy till you've combined the flour..
  7. Pour mixture into your loaf tin and bake for one hour. Fifty minutes for fan ovens. Or until firm to touch and a skewer comes clean after being inserted into the middle of the loaf..
  8. Remove from the oven and leave for ten minutes before removing from the loaf tin and placing on a wire rack to cool..
