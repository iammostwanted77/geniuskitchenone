Ingredients of Ashlee's Green Bean Casserole

  1. You need 1 can of Campbell's cream of mushroom soup.
  2. You need 3/4 cup of Milk.
  3. You need 1/8 tsp of black pepper.
  4. It's 2 can of cut green beans, drained.
  5. It's 1 1/3 cup of French's Original French Fried Onions.

Ashlee's Green Bean Casserole step by step

  1. Mix soup, milk, and black pepper in a 1.5 quart baking dish..
  2. Stir in green beans and 2/3 cup of the French's Original French Fried Onions..
  3. Bake in oven at 350° for 30 minutes or until hot..
  4. Stir. Top with remaining 2/3 cup of onions..
  5. Bake an additional 5 minutes or until onions are golden brown..
