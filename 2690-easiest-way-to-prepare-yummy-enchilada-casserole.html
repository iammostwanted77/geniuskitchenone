Ingredients of Enchilada Casserole

  1. Prepare 1 lb of Ground Beef.
  2. You need 1/2 of Onion (chopped).
  3. You need 1 tsp of Salt.
  4. It's 1/4 tsp of Pepper.
  5. It's 1 Tbsp of Chili Powder.
  6. You need 1/2 can of Tomato Sauce.
  7. It's 1/4 c of Butter.
  8. Prepare 1 1/2 c of Cheese (shredded).
  9. You need 6-8 of White Corn Tortillas.

Enchilada Casserole step by step

  1. Brown ground beef and onions in large frying pan..
  2. Add in salt, pepper, chili powder, and tomato sauce..
  3. Rub butter around casserole dish..
  4. Butter tortillas. Put 2 tortillas on bottom of casserole dish. Cover the tortillas with 1/3 meat mixture, 1/3 cheese, and 1/3 olives..
  5. Put 2 tortillas onto of the above layer and add 1/3 meat mixture, 1/3 cheese, and 1/3 olives..
  6. Repeat step 5..
  7. Pour 1 cup water on top and put foil over pan (or casserole lid)..
  8. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes..
