Recipe: Delicious Quinoa Chili

Quinoa Chili.

You can have Quinoa Chili using 20 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you cook that.

Ingredients of Quinoa Chili

  1. You need 1/2 cup of uncooked quinoa.
  2. You need 1 tbsp of extra virgin olive oil.
  3. You need 1 of large yellow onion, diced.
  4. Prepare 4 cloves of garlic, minced.
  5. It's 1 can of diced tomatoes (28 oz).
  6. It's 1 can of can tomato sauce (15 oz).
  7. You need 1 1/2 cups of water.
  8. It's 1 can of diced green chiles (7 oz).
  9. Prepare 2 1/2 tbsp of chili powder.
  10. Prepare 2 tsp of ground cumin.
  11. Prepare 2 tsp of cocoa powder.
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp of paprika.
  13. You need 1/2 tsp of granulated sugar.
  14. Prepare 1/2 tsp of ground coriander.
  15. You need 1/2 tsp of cayenne pepper.
  16. Prepare to taste of salt and black pepper.
  17. You need 2 cans of red kidney beans, drained and rinsed (15 oz).
  18. Prepare 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed (15 oz).
  19. It's 2 tbsp of cilantro.
  20. Prepare 1 of juice of lime.

Quinoa Chili step by step

  1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions..
  2. Heat olive oil in a large enameled cast iron pot over medium-high heat..
  3. Once oil is hot add onion and saute until tender, about 4 minutes, adding in garlic during last 30 seconds of sauteing..
  4. Add in diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, cooked quinoa, water, green chiles, chili powder, cumin, cocoa, paprika, sugar, coriander, cayenne pepper, and season with salt and pepper to taste..
  5. Bring mixture just to a boil, then reduce heat to simmer, cover pot, and allow to simmer 30 minutes..
  6. Add in all beans, cilantro, and lime juice and cook until heated through..
  7. Serve warm with optional toppings and sides (cheddar, sour cream, diced avocados, etc.).
