Bake tacos step by step

  1. Brown hamburger meat, drain off any grease. Add taco seasoning, follow instructions on packet. Set aside.
  2. Heat up beans in microwave so it’s easier to work with..
  3. Put refried beans on bottom of taco shell, add some meat, then cheese. Places all tacos in a 13x9 inc baking dish..
  4. Bake on 350 until cheese has melted. When done top with lettuce and salsa verde. (and any other topping you like).
