Ingredients of Chili macaroni

  1. It's 1 lb of hamburger.
  2. Prepare 1 of large onion diced.
  3. It's 1 of large tomato diced.
  4. It's 1 can of tomato soup.
  5. Prepare 1 can of black beans.
  6. It's of Macaroni noodles 2 cups dry.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of Red pepper flakes.
  8. You need to taste of Chili powder.
  9. Prepare 2 tsp of oil.
  10. You need 3 cups of water.

Chili macaroni instructions

  1. Cook hamburger til no longer pink. Drain and place back in pan..
  2. Add oil and heat up again. When hot add onion,tomato,red pepper flakes, and some chili powder. Cook till onions and tomatos are soft..
  3. Add tomato soup, water,black beans,and macaroni..
  4. Turn on simmer and add some more chili powder. Cook til noodles are soft and liquid is soaked up..
  5. You can add more water if it turns out too thick or cheese to make it creamier..
