Wifey’s Chicken Tacos instructions

  1. Boil and shred chicken, save some of the chicken stalk.
  2. Mince and sauté onion with butter in a large pan.
  3. Add chicken, tomato sauce, and a bit of chicken stalk to pan over medium heat until moist consistency.
  4. Add knorr to taste and let simmer until flavors mix, about 5 mins.
  5. Lightly fry tortillas on separate pan greased with butter.
  6. Scoop chicken onto tortillas and sprinkle with Cojita cheese.
