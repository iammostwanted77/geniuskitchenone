Ingredients of EASY BANANA BREAD

  1. It's 3 1/2 cup of All Purpose Flour.
  2. Prepare 3 cup of Sugar.
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of Baking Soda.
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of Salt.
  5. You need 1 tsp of Baking Powder.
  6. You need 1 tsp of Ground Cinnamon.
  7. You need 1 tsp of Vanilla.
  8. It's 4 of Eggs.
  9. It's 2/3 cup of Water.
  10. Prepare 1 cup of Vegetable Oil.
  11. You need 2 cup of Bananas mashed.
  12. You need 1 cup of Pecans, Chopped.

EASY BANANA BREAD instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°, prepare baking pans I spray with Pam cooking spray w/flour.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Mix together on medium speed of with electric mixer..
  3. Bake for 1 hour or until firm and golden brown.
  4. Cool on wire rack for at least 15 minutes before turning out on tray..
