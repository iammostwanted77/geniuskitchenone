You can cook Vegetable Chili! using 13 ingredients and 5 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Vegetable Chili!

  1. It's 2 packets of Simply Organic Vegetarian Chili seasoning mix.
  2. Prepare 2 (15 oz) of cans organic black beans.
  3. It's 2 (15 oz) of cans organic garbanzo beans.
  4. Prepare 2 (28 oz) of cans organic whole peeled tomatoes.
  5. Prepare 1 of zucchini.
  6. You need 1 of yellow squash.
  7. Prepare 2 cups of kale.
  8. It's of Avocado.
  9. Prepare of Yellow onion.
  10. Prepare of Jalapeño peppers.
  11. It's of Sea salt.
  12. You need 1 tbsp of Cayenne pepper.
  13. Prepare of Water.

Vegetable Chili! step by step

  1. Chop and sauté the zucchini, yellow squash and kale with some of the seasoning.
  2. Put everything else in a pot.
  3. Mix the rest of the seasonings in warm water until they dissolve and then add sauteed veggies and seasoning to pot mixture.
  4. Add water as needed, bring to boil and the lower heat to simmer for about 15-20 minutes..
  5. Chop onions, and avocados for toppings. Use jalapeño as toppings..
