Fully loaded Chilli concarne step by step

  1. Brown the mince and add the chillies to taste.
  2. Roughly chop uo the vedge.
  3. Add the vegetables.
  4. Add the chilli mix to 300ml of cold water add to the mince along with the tins of tomatoes and beans..
  5. Add the kidney beans and crushed garlic.
  6. Fry up up the chorizo in some olive oil and add to the chilli.
  7. Cook through for 40 mins and serve with rice and some crusty bread.
