Taco Stuffed Zucchini Boats step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 400..
  2. Half and hollow out zucchini leaving 1/4" thick. Set aside 3/4 cup for taco mixture..
  3. Boil zucchini halves for 1 minute.
  4. Brown turkey, add spices and mix well..
  5. Add onion, pepper, zucchini, tomato sauce and water. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes..
  6. Put 1/4 cup salsa in bottom of baking dish..
  7. Fill zucchini halves with taco mixture. Top with cheese..
  8. Cover with foil and bake 25 minutes..
  9. Serve with cilantro and salsa..
