Ingredients of Brownie Banana Bread

  1. It's 3-4 of very ripe bananas.
  2. Prepare 1/3 cup of melted butter.
  3. It's 1 teaspoon of baking soda.
  4. It's 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
  5. It's 1 1/2 cups of granulated sugar.
  6. Prepare 1 of large egg beaten.
  7. It's 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of corn oil.
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of all purpose flour.
  10. Prepare 3/4 cup of Hershey's Cocoa.
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup of Hershey's chocolate syrup.

Brownie Banana Bread instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 and butter a 4x8 loaf pan.
  2. Mash banana and add melted butter.
  3. Mix in baking soda, salt, sugar, egg, vanilla extract, flour, oil, and cocoa.
  4. Add chocolate syrup and mix well.
  5. Put into loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes.
  6. Serve warm or let cool..
  7. OPTIONAL: Add nuts if desired.
  8. Serving suggestion: Slice banana over each slice and dust with powdered sugar for a decadent dessert.
