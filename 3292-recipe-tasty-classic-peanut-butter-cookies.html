Ingredients of Classic Peanut Butter Cookies

  1. It's 1 cup of Unsalted butter.
  2. You need 1 cup of Crunchy peanut butter.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of White sugar.
  4. Prepare 1 cup of Packed brown sugar.
  5. It's 2 of Eggs.
  6. You need 2 1/2 cup of All-purpose flour.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of Baking powder.
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Salt.
  9. It's 1 1/2 tsp of Baking soda.

Classic Peanut Butter Cookies step by step

  1. Cream together butter, peanut butter,& sugars. Beat in eggs.
  2. In a seperate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, & salt. Stir into batter.
  3. Put batter in refrigerator for 1 hour.
  4. Roll into 1 inch balls & put on baking sheets.
  5. Flatten each ball with a fork, making a criss-cross pattern.
  6. Bake in a preheated 375Â°F oven for about 10 minutes or until cookies begin to brown. Don't overbake.
