Pumpkin banana bread step by step

  1. Preheat oven 350° Fahrenheit.
  2. Melt the butter. Mix the bread mix together adding the bananas last I used a mixer with this.
  3. Mix the topping ingredients together make sure the butter is melted but not to hot.
  4. Add the bread ingredients to a deep pie or cake pan add topping mixture to top bake in the oven.
  5. Bake for one hour let cool serve hope you enjoy.
