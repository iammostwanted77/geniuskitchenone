Ingredients of Mango icecream

  1. Prepare 2 cup of matka kulfi (already posted).
  2. It's 2 cup of mango pulp.
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of milk powder.
  4. You need 2 tbsp of cream.
  5. It's 2 tbsp of mango chunks.

Mango icecream step by step

  1. In a mixture jar take mango pulp, milk powder and cream. Blend it well..
  2. Divide mango pulp into two parts. (¼ and ¾).
  3. Add mango chunks in one part (¾ one) and mix well. Keep aside..
  4. Mix second half (¼ one) in matka kulfi mixture. Mix it slightly..
  5. Pour matka kulfi mixture into box.now pour mango chunks mixture over it. Now stir it with a spoon so we get "Zebra design" on icecream. Freeze it overnight..
  6. Before scoop icecream, put it on room temperature for 10 minutes. So we easily scoop it..
