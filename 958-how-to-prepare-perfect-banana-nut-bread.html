Ingredients of Banana Nut Bread

  1. You need 2 cups of sugar.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of butter (2 sticks), softened.
  3. You need 2 cups of flour.
  4. You need 5 of eggs.
  5. It's 6 Tbsp. of milk.
  6. It's 2 tsp. of baking soda.
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cups of chopped ripe banana (about 3 bananas).
  8. It's 1 cup of chopped walnuts.

Banana Nut Bread step by step

  1. Place all ingredients into mixer..
  2. Blend well; allow to sit at room temperature for 1 hour..
  3. Split equally among 2 greased bread pans..
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes. Allow to completely cool..
  5. Once cooked wrap in plastic wrap and place in ziplock bag. I store mine in fridge and freeze some for later..
  6. Enjoy!.
