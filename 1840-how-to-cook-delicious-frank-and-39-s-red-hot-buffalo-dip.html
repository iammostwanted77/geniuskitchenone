Ingredients of Frank's Red Hot Buffalo Dip

  1. You need 8 oz of Package of Cream Cheese, Softened.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of Ranch Salad Dressing.
  3. It's 1/2 cup of Frank's Red Hot Sauce Original.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of Shredded Mozzarella Cheese.
  5. Prepare 2 can of SWANSON� White Premium Chunk Chicken Breast in Water, drained.

Frank's Red Hot Buffalo Dip instructions

  1. HEAT oven to 350°F. Place cream cheese into deep baking dish. Stir until smooth.
  2. MIX in salad dressing, Frank's RedHot Sauce and cheese. Stir in chicken..
  3. BAKE 20 min. or until mixture is heated through; stir. Garnish as desired. Serve with crackers or vegetables..
