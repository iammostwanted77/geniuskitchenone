Green Bean Casserole instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350.
  2. empty cans of green beans into pot (keeping the juice from the cans).
  3. cook on low.
  4. while green beans are simmering lightly cook bacon with chopped onions.
  5. cook bacon until slightly cooked then add bacon chopped onions and bacon grease to green beans and cook for another 5 mins.
  6. when done drain juices and add to desired pan.
  7. add cream of potato evenly through out the dish once done add dried onions to the top and bake for 20 mins.
