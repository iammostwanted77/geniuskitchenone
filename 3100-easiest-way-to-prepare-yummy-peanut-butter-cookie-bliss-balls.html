Ingredients of Peanut-Butter Cookie bliss balls

  1. Prepare 4 Tbsp of Peanut-Butter.
  2. Prepare 3 Tbsp of honey.
  3. You need 1 pinch of salt.
  4. Prepare 1 tsp of vanilla essence.
  5. Prepare 1 1/4 cups of ground almond.
  6. You need 40 grams of chocolate (chips or chopped).

Peanut-Butter Cookie bliss balls step by step

  1. Mix together the P-B, salt, honey and vanilla until combined.
  2. Put in the almond and knead with your fingers until it forms a dough. Add chocolate and mix until incorparated..
  3. Shape the mixture into balls and put in the fridge to set. Keep the bliss balls in the fridge..
