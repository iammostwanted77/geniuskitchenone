Ingredients of Taco Tuesday

  1. It's 1 lb of ground beef, browned and drained.
  2. You need 1 tsp of each chili powder, cumin, dry mustard,garlic and onion powders, salt, pepper, paprika, cornstarch.
  3. It's 1/2-1 cup of water.
  4. You need 1/2 lb of shredded cheese, I used Monterrey Jack.
  5. Prepare of Crispy taco shells.
  6. Prepare of Sour cream.
  7. Prepare of Shredded lettuce.
  8. You need of Taco sauce.

Taco Tuesday instructions

  1. Brown and drain your ground beef. In a measuring cup, combine all your spices and stir. Add to beef and stir. Add water and cook for a few minutes stirring..
  2. Heat your oven to 350. On an inverted muffin tin, line up your taco shells, 2 per row. Heat til crispy and warm. Remove from oven. Fill each shell with meat, cheese, lettuce, and sour cream, in that order. Take it to the table. The rest is history..
