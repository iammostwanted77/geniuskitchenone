Easy Meatloaf Patties step by step

  1. preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. mix beef, stuffing mix, water, salt and onion together.
  3. pat mix into hamburger like patties and put on cookie sheet.
  4. place in preheated oven for 25-30 minutes and enjoy!.
  5. if you don't have any stuffing mix you can substitute 3/4 pkg saltine crackers and one egg..
