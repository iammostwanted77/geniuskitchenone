Meatloaf with glaze instructions

  1. Optional step In a skillet sautee onion, celery, red peppers, garlic and carrots in butter and olive oil till tender..
  2. Pre heat oven to 375 degrees.
  3. Mix everything well except topping.
  4. Form into a loaf in a 13×9 inch dish.
  5. Bake for 1 hour and 20 minutes or till done. Mix up glaze topping and set aside..
  6. Pour some sauce on and continue to bake for at least 20 more minutes.
  7. Let rest before slicing and serve with additional sauce as desired.
