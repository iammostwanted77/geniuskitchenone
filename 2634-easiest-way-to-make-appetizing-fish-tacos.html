Fish tacos instructions

  1. Make long thin strips of fish fillets and marinate with cayanne pepper, cumin powder, garlic powder, salt, lemon juice and set aside for 30 mins.
  2. Heat oil in a skillet and pan fry marinated fish fillets until nicely browned on the outside. Set aside.
  3. Heat up the flour tortilla until warm..
  4. Place the tortilla in a plate and top with fish fillets, guacamole, salsa, shredded Mexican cheese and cilantro..
