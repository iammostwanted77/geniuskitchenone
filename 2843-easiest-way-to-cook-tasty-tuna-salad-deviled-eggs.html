Ingredients of Tuna Salad Deviled Eggs

  1. Prepare 6 of Eggs. I used.
  2. You need 1 can of drained tuna fish.
  3. You need 2 tablespoons of mayo.
  4. You need 1 teaspoon of mustard.
  5. You need of Diced celery to your liking.
  6. It's of Diced onion to your liking.
  7. Prepare 2 tablespoons of sweet or dill relish.
  8. Prepare to taste of Salt and pepper.

Tuna Salad Deviled Eggs step by step

  1. Hard boil eggs. Cool and peel..
  2. While eggs are cooling, mix drained tuna, onion, relish, celery, salt, pepper.
  3. Mix in mayo and mustard. Can use more or less.
  4. Cut eggs in half and mix the yolks into the tuna salad..
  5. Stuff egg whites with tuna salad like you would for deviled eggs..
