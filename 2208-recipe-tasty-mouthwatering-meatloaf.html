Mouthwatering Meatloaf step by step

  1. Combine ground beef, sausage, onion, soup mix, eggs, and breadcrumbs in a bowl..
  2. Add shredded cheese and mix together..
  3. Spray a shallow baking dish with non-stick spray..
  4. Form mixture into an oval in pan..
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 350Â°F for 45 minutes..
  6. Remove foil and continue baking for another 10-15 minutes as necessary..
  7. Remove and let cool 10 minutes..
  8. Slice into thick pieces and add to your mashed potatoes..
  9. Make gravy as directed. Pour over and enjoy!.
