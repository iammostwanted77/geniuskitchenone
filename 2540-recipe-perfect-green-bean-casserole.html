Green bean casserole! (: step by step

  1. preheat oven to 359°.
  2. mix cream of mushroom soup and milk together.
  3. add green beans to soup mix.
  4. mix in about half of french fried onions in green beans.
  5. put in an 8x8 baking dish.
  6. bake until boiling.
  7. top with the remaining french fried onions and bake for about 5-10 mins.
  8. let cool.
  9. enjoy! (:.
