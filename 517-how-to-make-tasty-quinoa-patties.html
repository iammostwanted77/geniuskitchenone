Ingredients of Quinoa patties

  1. Prepare 2.5 cups of cooked quinoa, room temp.
  2. Prepare 3-4 of large eggs.
  3. You need 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  4. It's to taste of spices (pepper, etc).
  5. It's 1/2 cup of chives.
  6. It's 3 cloves of garlice, chopped.
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup of gruyere or parmessian cheese.
  8. You need of ~1 cup bread crumbs.
  9. It's 1 tablespoon of olive oil or butter.

Quinoa patties instructions

  1. Mix quinoa, salt, and eggs in a large bowl.
  2. Stir in the chives, cheese, garlic, and spices.
  3. Add bread crumbs until the mixture is firm enough to form into patties.
  4. Make approx. 12 patties. Smaller is better.
  5. Heat oil over medium low heat in a frying pan.
  6. Cook ~6 patties at a time, 7-10 minutes per side (until golden brown).
  7. Remove the cooked patties, place on a wire rack to cool, and cook the rest.
