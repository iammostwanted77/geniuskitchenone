Ingredients of Banana bread

  1. It's 4 of bananas.
  2. It's 125 g of butter.
  3. Prepare 150 g of brown sugar.
  4. Prepare 175 g of flour.
  5. Prepare 2 of eggs.
  6. You need 1 of table spoon of cinnamon.
  7. It's of little bit of nutmeg.
  8. Prepare 1 of table spoon of baking powder.
  9. You need 50 g of cashews.
  10. Prepare 70 g of chocolate chips.
