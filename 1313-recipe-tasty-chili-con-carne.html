Ingredients of Chili con carne

  1. It's 400 gr of ground beef.
  2. Prepare 1 of onion.
  3. Prepare 3 of garlic.
  4. It's 2 of Chopped tomatoes.
  5. Prepare 1 tsp of oregano.
  6. Prepare 2 tsp of paprika powder.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of chilli powder.
  8. You need 1 tsp of black pepper.
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp of cinnamon.
  10. It's 1/2 tsp of cumin.
  11. It's 1 of chopped red paprika.
  12. You need 3 tbsp of tomato puree.
  13. Prepare 250 gr of kidney beans.
  14. You need 1/4 tsp of beef stock.
  15. It's of Oil and salt.

Chili con carne instructions

  1. Heat the oil over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic, saute it until fragrant.
  3. Add Ground beef, break it up with your spoon or spatula, and stir it well.
  4. Stir in oregano, paprika powder, chilli powder, black pepper, cinnamon, and cumin.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, some water, stir it, and let it boil.
  6. Stir in chopped paprika, tomato purée, kidney beans, salt and beef stock.
  7. Let it boil until thickened, and stir it occasionally.
