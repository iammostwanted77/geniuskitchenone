Ingredients of Delish Peanut Butter Cookies

  1. You need 1 cup of Packed brown sugar.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of Softened butter.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of Creamy peanut butter.
  4. It's 1 of Egg.
  5. It's 1 tsp of Vanilla.
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of All purpose flour.
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Baking powder.
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Baking soda.
  9. You need 1/2 tsp of Salt.
  10. It's 1/2 tsp of Sugar. I just poured some sugar on an extra plate to gently roll cookie balls in before putting in oven..

Delish Peanut Butter Cookies instructions

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Combine brown sugar, butter and peanut butter in a bowl. Beat until mixed. Add the egg and vanilla. Beat again until well mixed. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Beat again until mixed..
  3. Shape dough into one inch balls. Roll the ball around in that extra sugar that you put on a separate plate. Place on an ungreased cookie sheet 2inches apart or so. Flatten the balls with a fork dipped in sugar and do a criss cross pattern..
  4. Bake 7-10 minutes..
