Ingredients of Easy Spaghetti Squash

  1. Prepare 1 of Spaghetti Squash, cut in half and seeded.
  2. It's 1/4 cup of butter or margarine.
  3. Prepare 1 small of onion.
  4. Prepare 2 clove of garlic finely chopped.
  5. It's 1 tsp of oregano.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of parsley.

Easy Spaghetti Squash instructions

  1. Set oven at 350 F, place squash on baking sheet, halves cut-side down, (I put a bit of water in the pan to help with the steaming proccess).
  2. Bake for 40 minutes.
  3. Once cool enough to handle shred squash using a fork.
  4. Melt butter over medium heat; add onion, garlic, oregano and parsley. Cook until onion and garlic are soft. Mix in squash and cook until hot..
