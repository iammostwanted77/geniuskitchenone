Ingredients of Easy Crockpot Spaghetti Squash & Meatballs

  1. Prepare 1 packages of Frozen meatballs.
  2. Prepare 1 packages of (Jar) of spaghetti sauce (we like Newmans sauce)..
  3. Prepare 1 each of Spaghetti squash-cut in half.

Easy Crockpot Spaghetti Squash & Meatballs step by step

  1. Spray crock pot with non-stick cooking spray..
  2. Cut squash in half and take out the seeds. Place both halves side by side in crock pot with the cut side down..
  3. Pour the jar of sauce and meatballs over the squash. Stir to combine the meatballs and sauce together..
  4. Cook on low for 6-8 hours..
  5. Once it's done cooking, take out the squash and scrape/shred the insides out with a fork. The squash should be very tender. It will resemble spaghetti noodles when shredded. Discard the rind of the squash..
  6. Top the shredded squash with the sauce and meatballs or you can add the squash directly to the sauce mixture in the pot..
