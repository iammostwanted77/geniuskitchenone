Ingredients of Rita's Bacon Meatloaf

  1. You need 2 lb of ground beef.
  2. It's 1 medium of onion.
  3. You need 2 medium of eggs.
  4. Prepare 1 cup of breadcrumbs ( i used crushed RITZ crackers).
  5. It's 1/3 cup of ketchup.
  6. Prepare 1/3 cup of A-1 steak sauce.
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of shredded cheese ( i used sharp cheddar.
  8. Prepare 6 slice of bacon.

Rita's Bacon Meatloaf instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F..
  2. Combine ground beef, eggs, ketchup, breadcrumbs, onions and 1 cup of cheese..
  3. Mix well..
  4. Form the meat mixture into a loaf shape. Place it in a casserole dish.
  5. Sprinkle the remaining half cup of shredded cheese atop the meatloaf..
  6. Drape the bacon over the meatloaf, overlapping slightly. Tuck the ends beneath the loaf..
  7. Pour A-1 sauce over bacon covering the meatloaf..
  8. Bake for approximately 90 minutes at 350°F or until internal temperature reads 160°F..
  9. Allow meatloaf to cool for a bit before slicing..
