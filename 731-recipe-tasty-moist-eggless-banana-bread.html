Moist eggless banana bread instructions

  1. Mash 1 ripe banana with a fork until there are no lumps..
  2. Mix 1/8 cup melted butter, 3 tablespoons aqua faba (or 1 egg) and 1/2 cup sugar to form a smooth liquid..
  3. Mix together 3/4 cup flour, 1 pinch salt and 1/2 teaspoon baking soda..
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients through a sieve. Fold until the flour is incorporated. Add the chocolate chips..
  5. Bake in a loaf pan in a pre-heated oven at 325 F for 55 minutes..
