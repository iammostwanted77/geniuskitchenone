Ingredients of Southern Style Thanksgiving Deviled Eggs!

  1. It's 1 of Boiled eggs (how much ever are required, I used 9).
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp of Wickles Relish.
  3. You need 1 tbsp of mayonnaise.
  4. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of yellow mustard.
  5. Prepare 1 of paprika.
  6. You need of salt and pepper.

Southern Style Thanksgiving Deviled Eggs! step by step

  1. Cut hard boiled eggs into halves, separate the yolks in a bowl and arrange the eggs in a plate..
  2. With a fork mash the yolks and add the relish, mayonnaise and mustard to the yolks, forming a creamy paste. I used nine eggs in this recipe and all the ingredients were added accordingly. Increase the quantity of relish, mayonnaise and mustard if using more eggs..
  3. Add salt and pepper, to taste, to the yolks..
  4. Add a dollop of the yolk mixture to each egg and sprinkle some paprika on each egg. Delicious Thanksgiving deviled eggs are ready!.
