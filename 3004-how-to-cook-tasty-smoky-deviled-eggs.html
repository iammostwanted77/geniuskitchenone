Ingredients of Smoky Deviled Eggs

  4. Prepare 2 tbsp of dijon mustard.

  6. It's 1 1/2 tsp of tabasco.
  7. You need 1 dash of liquid smoke.
  8. It's 1 of sea salt.
  9. You need 1 of pepper.
  10. Prepare 1 of sweet paprika.

Smoky Deviled Eggs instructions

  1. Boil eggs, cool, peel & cut in half (scoop out yolks).
  2. place smoked chicken in food processor & process until finely ground.
  3. add yolks, and process.
  4. add mayo, mustard, worcestershire, tabasco, and liquid smoke, pulse to mix.
  5. if dry, add more mayo.
  6. season with salt & pepper, stuff into egg halves and sprinkle with paprika.
