Ingredients of All Star Deviled Eggs

  1. Prepare 6 of hard boiled eggs (peeled).
  2. It's 1/2 cup of mayo (a little more or less depending on desired creaminess).
  3. You need 1 tsp of dijon or yellow mustard.
  4. It's 1 of nice squeeze of lemon.
  5. It's 1 dash of salt.
  6. You need 1 dash of black pepper.
  7. You need 1 dash of cayenne for added kick if desired.
  8. Prepare 1 dash of green onion OR fresh dill as garnish.
  9. It's 2 dash of paprika as garnish.

All Star Deviled Eggs step by step

  1. Cut eggs in half length wise..
  2. Spoon out egg yokes into a bowl.
  3. Mash yokes and add the mayo, mustard, lemon juice, salt, pepper and cayenne if some spiciness is desired. Mash and mix till nice and creamy. I like to do a taste test before I'm done to see if the tartness and taste is how I desire..
  4. Return the creamy mixture into the eggs and garnish with sliced green onions or fresh dill. Enjoy! :-).
