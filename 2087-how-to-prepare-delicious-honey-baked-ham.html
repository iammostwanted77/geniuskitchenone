Honey baked ham step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Place ham in foil lined roasting pan. Bake the rounded side up for 1 hour.
  3. in a medium saucepan over medium heat mix all ingredients together. Stir until it forms a thick glaze.
  4. Rub on half of the glaze and bake for 30 minutes more. flip the ham over and rub on the remaining glaze and bake for 1 hour more. Remove the ham and allow to rest for 30 minutes before serving.
