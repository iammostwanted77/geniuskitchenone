Ingredients of Meatloaf with Sweet & Spicy Glaze

  1. Prepare 1 pound of hamburger.
  2. You need 1 of egg.
  3. It's 1 of small onion, diced.
  4. Prepare 1/2 of small bell pepper, diced.
  5. It's 1 cup of bread crumbs.
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon of onion powder.
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon of garlic powder.
  8. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon of salt.
  9. It's to taste of Pepper.
  10. You need of Glaze:.
  11. You need 1 cup of ketchup.
  12. Prepare 2 tablespoons of brown sugar.
  13. You need 1/8 teaspoon of cayenne pepper.
  14. It's of To taste, Black pepper.

Meatloaf with Sweet & Spicy Glaze instructions

  1. Mix ketchup, brown sugar and spices until well combined..
  2. In large bowl mix hamburger, onion, pepper and spices..
  3. Add egg and bread crumbs. Mix until well combined and incorporated..
  4. Place in 9x9 or loaf pan..
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes. Drain oil and pour glaze over meatloaf..
  6. Return to over and cook another 15 minutes or until thoroughly cooked inside..
