non bake cocoa-peanut butter cookies instructions

  1. mix sugar, cocoa and milk bring to boil, boil 4 minutes.
  2. In seperate bowl, put in your oats, mix peanut butter and vanilla, set in bowl on oats.
  3. pour boiled cocoa over peanut butter and oats, mix until fully covered.
  4. drop by spoonful onto wax paper quickly, they will set fast.
  5. no need to make a fuss over how spaced they are, they do not expand...make em as big, or small as you wish :).
  6. They don't take very long to set, so be careful, they are addictive and you won't wanna share.
