Buffalo Chicken Wing Dip step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 400dg..
  2. Pan fry chicken. Season if you prefer..
  3. Set chicken aside on paper towel to cool and drain grease..
  4. Combine in a pan Red Hot, Ranch, Cheddar Cheese and (Room Temp.) cream cheese. Best to whisk till smooth and creamy..
  5. Add Chicken and simmer while stirring occasionally for about 10 mins..
  6. In a baking dish add the mixture, top with Shredded cheese.
  7. Bake 20 mins.Then let stand..
