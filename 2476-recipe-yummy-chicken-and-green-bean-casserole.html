Chicken and Green Bean Casserole step by step

  1. Boil, let cool, and peel the eggs.
  2. Preheat oven 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  3. Add the cubed chicken to pan, cover and simmer..
  4. Chop the eggs and mix with crackers, and the five cheese mixture, set aside in a bowl.
  5. When the chicken is almost done, add the salt, garlic and beans. Cover and let cook 15 minutes.
  6. Add raw eggs to cracker cheese mixture then mix well.
  7. Remove the casserole from the heat. Break up the bread and add milk, and mix well.
  8. Add topping and put into oven for 30 minutes.
  9. Cover and let sit 7 minutes. Serve I hope you enjoy!.
