Ravioli lasagna recipe step by step

  1. In a large skillet, cook beef over medium heat until no longer pink. Drain..
  2. In a greased 2-1/2-qt. Baking dish, layer a third of the spaghetti sauce sauce, half of the ravioli and beef and 1/2 cup cheese..
  3. Repeat layers..
  4. Top with remaining sauce and cheese..
  5. Cover and bake at 400° for 40-45 minutes or until heated through..
