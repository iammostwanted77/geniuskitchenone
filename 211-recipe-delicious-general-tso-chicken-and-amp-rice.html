Ingredients of General Tso Chicken & Rice

  1. Prepare 1 lb of chicken breast, cubed.
  2. It's of Oil for frying.
  3. You need 2 Tbsp of minced ginger.
  4. You need 1/2 Tsp of red chilli flakes.
  5. Prepare 2 cloves of garlic minced.
  6. You need 3 Tbs of rice vinegar.
  7. You need 3 Tbs of soy sauce.
  8. It's 2 Tsp of hoisin sauce.
  9. You need 1/4 cup of water.
  10. It's 3 Tbs of sugar.
  11. It's 1/4 cup of white flour.
  12. You need 3/4 cup of jasmine rice.

General Tso Chicken & Rice step by step

  1. Toss the chicken with the quarter cup of flour and let sit while you mix the sauce ingredients..
  2. Put your jasmine rice in a pot with water and cook as directed on package..
  3. Add the rice vinegar, soy sauce, hoisin sauce, water, and sugar in a small bowl and whisk together..
  4. Add the chicken to a pan with the oil and fry until crispy..
  5. Remove chicken from the pan..
  6. In a small pan add a tablespoon of the oil and add the chilli flakes, ginger and garlic. Cook until you smell the garlic..
  7. Add the sauce mixture and stir until slightly thickens (a few minutes).
  8. In a large bowl add the chicken and toss with the thickened sauce..
  9. Serve immediately over rice..
