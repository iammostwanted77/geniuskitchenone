Ingredients of Cheesy Turkey Meatloaf

  1. It's 1 of large pack ground turkey.
  2. You need 1 cup of whole milk.
  3. You need 2 tablespoons of Worcestershire sauce.
  4. It's 1 cup of Italian seasoned bread crumbs.
  5. Prepare 2 of eggs.
  6. It's 3 tablespoons of oregano.
  7. It's 3 tablespoons of Italian seasoning.
  8. You need 3 tablespoons of garlic powder.
  9. Prepare 3 tablespoons of onion powder.
  10. Prepare 1 teaspoon of black pepper.
  11. It's 6 of mozzarella cheese sticks.
  12. It's of Provolone cheese (as many as you need).
  13. Prepare Jar of spaghetti sauce (your favorite flavor).
