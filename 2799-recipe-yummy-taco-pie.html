Ingredients of Taco Pie

  1. Prepare 1 pound of ground beef.
  2. Prepare 1 package of Pillsbury crescent rolls.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of sour cream.
  4. Prepare 2 cups of shredded Monterey Jack cheese.
  5. Prepare 1 cup of crushed nacho cheese Doritos.
  6. You need 1 packet of taco seasoning.

Taco Pie instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. Line a pie plate with the crescent rolls, pinch seams together to make your pie crust.
  3. Brown your beef and drain the grease.
  4. Add taco seasoning according to the instructions on the packet.
  5. Add your taco meat to the pie as the bottom layer.
  6. Spread a layer or sour cream on top of the beef.
  7. Generously cover the sour cream with a layer of shredded cheese.
  8. Add a layer of the crushed Doritos.
  9. Cover the rim of your pie plate with aluminum foil so your crust doesn’t burn.
  10. Bake for 20 minutes in your preheated oven.
  11. After the 20 minutes add another thin layer of shredded cheese and remove the foil to allow the crust to bake golden brown.
  12. Pop it back in the oven for 4 minutes then let it cool and serve with lettuce and salsa if you’d like.
