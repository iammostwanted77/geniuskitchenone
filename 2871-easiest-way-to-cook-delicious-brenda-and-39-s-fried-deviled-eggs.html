Ingredients of Brenda's Fried Deviled Eggs

  1. You need 12 large of hard cooked eggs.
  2. You need 1 cup of mayonnaise.
  3. It's 1/3 cup of Dijon mustard.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of paprika.
  5. It's 1/2 tbsp of cayenne.
  6. You need 1 1/2 tbsp of curry powder.
  7. You need 2 of lemons, juiced.
  8. It's to taste of Kosher salt.
  9. It's to taste of Ground Black pepper.
  10. Prepare 2 of eggs lightly whipped.
  11. It's 1 cup of corn starch.
  12. Prepare 2 cup of panko bread crumbs.
  13. Prepare of Crumbled, cooked bacon or other garnish (optional).

Brenda's Fried Deviled Eggs step by step

  1. Peel and slice eggs from top to bottom in half. Remove yolk and place whites to the side. Mash yolks, add the mayo, mustard, paprika, cayenne and curry powder and blend well till smooth. Season with salt, pepper and lemon juice to taste. Place filling into pastry bag using a small hole tip..
  2. First dip cooked egg whites in the corn starch and shake off any excess, then coat in whipped eggs. Next roll it in the panko bread crumbs, coating it well..
  3. Fry until the panko is golden brown. Place the cooked egg whites on a plate and fill with yolk filling. Garnish with crumbled, cooked bacon, or other garnish if desired..
