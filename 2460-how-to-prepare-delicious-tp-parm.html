TP Parm instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375.
  2. cut squash in half..scrap out seeds...place face down in dish with half inch water.
  3. put in oven for 50 minutes or until soft enough to put fork through skin easily.
  4. cover filets with Pam spray..both sides...sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  5. remove squash from oven...scrap out innards with fork into bowl. add butter, dill, salt and pepper.mix thoroughly.
  6. put filets on tray with foil.place in oven for 4 minutes on broil.
  7. flip and broil 4 minutes or until slight crisp of the edges.
  8. remove filets and cover with sauce and cheese.
  9. broil until cheese golden brown.
