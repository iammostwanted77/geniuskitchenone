Ingredients of Spaghetti squash with Turkey

  1. It's 1 of spaghetti squash.
  2. It's 1 lb of ground turkey.
  3. It's 3 cloves of garlic.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of chopped onions.
  5. You need 2 1/2 tablespoons of butter.
  6. You need 1/2 teaspoon of Italian seasoning.
  7. You need to taste of Season salt and pepper.
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup of - 1 cup Parmesan cheese (depending on size of squash).

Spaghetti squash with Turkey step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Cut squash in half and remove seeds. Lightly brush cut sides with olive oil and place cut side down in baking dish..
  3. Bake for 45-60 minutes. If you can easily pierce the skin of the squash with a knife it's done..
  4. While squash is baking cook the remaining ingredients EXCEPT for the Parmesan cheese together in a frying pan. And remove from heat..
  5. Once the squash is done, remove from oven and let it cool..
  6. Once cooled enough to be able to handle take a fork and scrape the inside out..
  7. Mix the squash with the meat mixture and add the Parmesan cheese. And return to the baking dish.
  8. Place back in the oven for 5 minutes or until cheese melts..
  9. The squash is supposed to be slightly crunchy. Enjoy!.
