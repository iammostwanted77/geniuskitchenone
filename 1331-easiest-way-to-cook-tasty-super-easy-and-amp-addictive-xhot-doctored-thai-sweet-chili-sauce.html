  2. You need 1 of very small clove of garlic, grated (about 1/2 teaspoon).
  3. You need 1-2 Tablespoons of minced hot chilies (I used really hot jalape침os and Thai chilies today).

  6. You need 1/3 cup of Thai sweet chilli sauce (I usually use Mae Ploy, but whatever brand you use is fine. You could also use Filipino Jufran Banana Sauce in a pinch.).
  7. You need 1-3 Tablespoons of fresh squeezed lime juice.
  8. It's 1/2 teaspoon of fish sauce.
