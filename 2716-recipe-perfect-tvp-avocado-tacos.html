TVP Avocado Tacos instructions

  1. In bowl combine TVP and HOT water. Stir and let sit..
  2. Heat oil in pan on medium.
  3. Add all other ingredients to bowl. Mix until incorporated..
  4. Add mixture to the pan. Stir occasionally. Cook uncovered for approximately 8 to 10 minutes to get the right consistency..
  5. Cut avocados in half and lay them open faced in the peel..
  6. Portion filling into the center of the avocado halves..
  7. Top with shredded cheese and diced tomatoes.
