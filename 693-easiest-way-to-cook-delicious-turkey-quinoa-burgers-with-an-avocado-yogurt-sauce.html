Ingredients of Turkey-Quinoa Burgers with an Avocado-Yogurt Sauce

  1. It's 2 of Tbs. extra-virgin olive oil.
  2. It's 4 of onion buns.
  3. Prepare of garlic salt.
  4. It's 1/4 cup of crumbled cotija cheese.
  5. Prepare of Burgers.
  6. It's 1 lb of ground turkey (or chicken).
  7. It's 3/4 cup of uncooked quinoa.
  8. Prepare 1/2 of red bell pepper, finely diced.
  9. You need 1 cup of cilantro leaves, divided in half.
  10. It's 1 tsp of chili powder.
  11. It's 1 tsp of cumin.
  12. You need 1 tsp of ground coriander.
  13. It's 1/2 tsp of garlic powder.
  14. You need 1 of coarse salt and freshly ground pepper.
  15. You need of Sauce.
  16. You need 1 large of avocado.
  17. You need 2 tbsp of plain Greek yogurt.
  18. It's 1 tbsp of juice from a lime.
  19. It's pinch of salt.

Turkey-Quinoa Burgers with an Avocado-Yogurt Sauce step by step

  1. In a food processor, combine the avocado, yogurt, 1/2 of the cilantro, lime juice and a pinch of salt until smooth like butter. Place in the fridge until time to serve. (I didn't have fresh cilantro so I subbed 1 tbsp dried cilantro.).
  2. Cook the quinoa in about 1 1/2 - 2 cups boiling water for about 12-15 minutes or until it pops into little spirals and water is absorbed..
  3. In a large bowl, combine the turkey, cooked quinoa, bell pepper, half the cilantro, chili powder, cumin, ground coriander (I didn't have any so I left it out), garlic powder, and a good pinch of salt and pepper. Thoroughly mix with your hands, and then form into four little patties. You can chill these in the fridge if you want, for as long as you want, or go ahead and cook ‘em!.
  4. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add the patties and sear until browned on both sides and cooked all the way through, 10 – 12 minutes total. Set aside..
  5. Lightly toast the onion buns brushed with olive oil and garlic salt..
  6. Smear a little of the avocado sauce on the bottom half of the onion bun. Top with a patty. Then a ton more avocado sauce. Then some crumbled cotija cheese. Then ask someone how to pronounce cotija...and then tell me. Lol! (I subbed queso fresco cuz I couldn't find it.).
  7. Excellent served with sweet potato fries and enjoy!.
