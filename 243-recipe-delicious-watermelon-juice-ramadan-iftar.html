Watermelon juice (Ramadan iftar) step by step

  1. Peel the watermelon and cut it into small pieces..
  2. In a grinder, add watermelon, water, lemon juice, jam-e-shirin and grind it..
  3. Now strain by using muslin cloth. Then add watermelon chunks and ice cubes..
  4. Watermelon Juice is ready to serve..
