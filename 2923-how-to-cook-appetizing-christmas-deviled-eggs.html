Christmas Deviled Eggs step by step

  1. Use as many eggs as you want to make and boil until hard boiled.
  2. Once cooking is done, remove from heat and rinse eggs with cold water to stop the cooking.
  3. Once eggs are cooled, peel and rinse all peeled eggs to get off any remaining shell pieces.
  4. Dry eggs well and cut in half as you would for deviled eggs.
  5. Place all yolks into the bowl. Add equal amounts of egg whites into the butter tubs, and fill with water until eggs are completely covered.
  6. Once whites are covered with water, add food coloring. As much or little as you like, and let soak for at least an hour..
  7. While they are soaking, add mayo, mustard, etc to make the filling to your liking, then cover until whites are done.
  8. After the hour is over, remove with slotted spoon and lay on paper towels to drain. Once they are drained and dry, fill with yolk mixture. Top with paprika, dill, green olives..its endless..
  9. Arrange in a pattern and serve immediately or cover with plastic and refrigerate until needed..
