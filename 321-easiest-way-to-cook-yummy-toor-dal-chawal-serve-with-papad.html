Toor dal chawal serve with papad step by step

  1. Take pot, put soaked toor dal,add red chilli powder, turmeric powder and fill half pot with water.
  2. Then after 40 minutes,check dal should be tender then add tomatoes..
  3. Cook it untill tomatoes becomes tender in dal. for about 20 minutes.
  4. Now when tomatoes becomes tender in dal add salt..
  5. Now take fry pan put oil,add zeera, chopped garlic and few whole red chillies..
  6. When garlic becomes slightly brown,whole red chilli colour slightly turn,add zeera..
  7. Put this tarka in dal..
  8. Add chopped hara dhania and few green chilies in it..
  9. Fry papad in oil.
  10. Toor dal is ready,serve with boiled white rice,cutted tomatoes and papad..
