Ingredients of peanut butter cookies .

  1. You need 125 grams of butter ..
  2. It's 3/4 cup of brown sugar ..
  3. You need 1/2 cup of castor sugar ..
  4. You need 1 of egg ..
  5. It's 1/2 cup of crunchy peanut butter ..
  6. It's 1/2 tsp of salt ..
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp of bicarbonate of soda ..
  8. You need 1/2 tsp of vanilla..
  9. Prepare 1 1/4 cup of plain flour ..

In a large bowl, cream together sugar and butter. Mix in peanut butter and vanilla until smooth and creamy. In a large bowl, mix all ingredients. Scoop level tablespoonfuls and roll into balls.

peanut butter cookies . step by step

  1. set oven at 180*c ,350*f . cream butter until soft ,then gradually beat in sugar and continue to beat until light and fluffy . add egg ,peanut butter ,salt and bicarbonate of soda and mix well ..
  2. add vanilla essence to mixture and blend in flour . roll mixture into balls and place on lightly greased baking sheet . flatten with floured fork ..
  3. bake for 10-12 minutes , until lightly golden . cool no wire sack ..
