best deviled eggs instructions

  1. boil eggs and let cool.
  2. cut eggs in half length wise and scoop out yokes.
  3. crush yokes with a fork until very fine or use a food processor.
  4. add vinegar, mustard, spices, and 6 heaping tbs of mao. u may have to add another tbs or two of mao. it depends on how creamy you like your filling.
  5. fill eggs with the yoke mixture and eat..
