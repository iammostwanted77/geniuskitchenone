  1. Prepare 2-3 of ripe avocados.
  2. Prepare 6 of cherry tomatoes finely chopped.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of chopped parsley.
  4. Prepare 1 cup of chopped onion.
  5. Prepare of Juice of 1 small lime.
  6. Prepare of Salt.

  8. Prepare of Garlic Powder.
  9. Prepare of Ground Cayenne red pepper.
