Ingredients of Quinoa vegetable cheesey eggy deliciousness

  1. You need 1 cup of quinoa.
  2. Prepare 2 of free range eggs.
  3. You need Half of head broccoli, cut into medium pieces.
  4. It's 15-20 of asparagus spears, cut into thirds.
  5. Prepare 5 leaves of kale, finely chopped.
  6. Prepare Half of a jar of salsa.
  7. Prepare 1 cup of (or more) cheddar cheese.
  8. Prepare 1 of avocado, cubed.
  9. It's 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  10. Prepare of Salt.
  11. Prepare of Pepper.

Quinoa vegetable cheesey eggy deliciousness instructions

  1. Cook the quinoa according to instructions.
  2. Steam the asparagus and broccoli for about 3 minutes so tender but still so they have some bite.
  3. On medium heat fry the chopped kale, cooked quinoa and steamed vegetables in olive oil for a few minutes.
  4. While this is cooking, poach the two eggs.
  5. Add salsa and cheese to the quinoa/vegetable mix and stir until heated and the cheese is melted (only a minute or so). Season well with salt and pepper..
  6. Divide the quinoa mix onto two plates, and place a poached egg on top of each..
  7. Add avocado cubes and some more salt and pepper.
