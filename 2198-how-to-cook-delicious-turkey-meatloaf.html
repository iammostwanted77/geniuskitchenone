Ingredients of Turkey meatloaf

  1. It's 1 lb of ground lean turkey.
  2. Prepare 1/2 tsp of marjoram.
  3. It's 1/2 tbsp of oregano.
  4. You need 1/2 tsp of basil.
  5. Prepare 1 bunch of small bunch of parsley, chopped finely.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of chopped onion, white or yellow for milder flavor..
  7. It's 4 clove of garlic minced.
  8. It's 1 cup of breadcrumbs or can use 1/2 cup rolled oats to be healthier.
  9. You need 5 of grinds of pepper or about 1 tsp black pepper.
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup of brown sugar or 1/2 cup applesauce to be healthier.
  11. You need 1 of large squeeze ketchup, about 1/3 cup.
  12. You need 1 of egg.
  13. It's 1/2 cup of diced or shredded carrots, for a different flavor, this is optional.
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup of diced celery, optional.
  15. You need 1/2 tsp of fresh or dried sage, optional...will add a thanksgiving flavor.

Turkey meatloaf step by step

  1. Put oven at 325, cook for one hour. May need longer, I sometimes do 85 minutes! I don't like it soo moist!.
  2. Mix all ingredients, add egg last before Turkey meat. Gently mix in Turkey meat..
  3. Cover top of meatloaf with enough ketchup to cover so you can't see the meat. Coat pan in olive oil..
  4. Shape loaf in bread pan and cover for first 30 minutes, second half the last 20-30 minutes leave uncovered..
  5. Make sure Turkey is cooked all the way through before you eat it!! It will be moist if you do egg n applesauce n ketchup in the mix!.
