Banana Bread with dry fruits instructions

  1. Mash bananas, add 2 lemon juice set aside.
  2. Flour and baking soda,buttermilk and salt in a medium bowl- set aside.
  3. Set oven to 275 F. Mix sugar egg and melted butter smoothly.
  4. Mix flour,with egg mixture.
  5. Add mashed bananas into the bowl.
  6. Add nuts, cranberries, apricots and figs.
  7. Spray baking dish with oil, pour batter.
  8. Cook 1 hour or over until tooth pick comes clean when you inserted into cakes.
  9. If you want serve with glaze (recipe is available in my pumpkin carrot cake recipe).
