spaghetti squash salad instructions

  1. cut spaghetti squash in half place in casserole dish flesh side up fill have of dish w/water cover with foil and cook in oven at 450 until tender about 40mins.
  2. after squash is cooked take a fork and scrap the inside flesh out length wise, it'll look just like spaghetti.
  3. during the summer I let squash chill and finish salad and serve cold and winter time I add ingredients right away and serve hot..
  4. chop onions, if you can find the mixed baby heirlooms just slice in half if you can only find large tomatoes dice. cut/rip purcuttio into bite size pieces.
  5. mix onions and percuttio with squash drizzle with olive oil, I'd say about 3 tblsps just as long as everything is coated. salt and pepper to taste. Enjoy!.
