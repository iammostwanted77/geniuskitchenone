Ingredients of Super soft chocolate chip peanut butter cookies

  1. It's 1/2 cup of salted butter, room temperature.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of light brown sugar.
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup of granulated sugar.
  4. Prepare 1 of large egg.
  5. You need 3/4 cup of creamy peanut butter.
  6. You need 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
  7. It's 1/2 tsp of baking soda.
  8. It's 1 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour.
  9. You need 1 1/2 cup of chocolate chips.

Super soft chocolate chip peanut butter cookies instructions

  1. Cream together butter and sugars.
  2. Mix in peanut butter, egg, and vanilla extract in that order..
  3. Slowly mix in baking soda and flour, don't over mix.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips with a wooden spoon.
  5. Chill for atleast 30 Minutes.

  7. Drop chilled dough by tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet and bake for 10-15 minutes.
