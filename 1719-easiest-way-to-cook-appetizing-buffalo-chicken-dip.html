Buffalo Chicken Dip.

You can have Buffalo Chicken Dip using 6 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Buffalo Chicken Dip

  1. It's 20 oz of Canned chicken (2 cans).
  2. You need 16 oz of Cream cheese (2 packages).
  3. You need 3/4 cup of Franks hot sauce.
  4. It's 1 cup of Ranch dressing.
  5. You need 2 cup of Mild cheddar cheese.
  6. You need 1 packages of Chips or bread to dip.

Buffalo Chicken Dip instructions

  1. Drain canned chicken and put in medium sauce pan.
  2. Add hot sauce and cook on medium until heated throughout..
  3. Add cream cheese and stir until blended thoroughly.
  4. Add ranch dressing and mix.
  5. Add 1-1/2 cups of cheddar cheese and heat until mixed and melted.
  6. Pour the dip into crock pot and sprinkle the remaining cheddar cheese on top.
