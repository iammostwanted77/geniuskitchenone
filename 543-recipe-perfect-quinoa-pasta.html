Quinoa Pasta.

You can have Quinoa Pasta using 4 ingredients and 14 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Quinoa Pasta

  1. It's 2 cups of quinoa.
  2. Prepare 2 TB of chia seeds.
  3. It's 6 TB of + 1/2 c warm water.
  4. It's 2 TB of olive oil.

Quinoa Pasta step by step

  1. Make Quinoa flour.
  2. Rinse quinoa and place on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 12 min on 200 until completely dry and a little crispy.
  4. Put through the omega using the blank screen until a powder forms. Use a sifter to remove the pieces that didn't crush well.
  5. I put it through the juicer 3 times to get as much as I can. Most of it crushed really well.
  6. Mix the chia seeds with 6 tablespoons of water until it gets thick. Amazing the way it works!! Got real awesome and goey.
  7. Add olive oil, flour and water to the chia seeds.
  8. Mix until a dough forms, shouldn't be too sticky.
  9. Attach pasta attachment of your choice to the blank screen of the omega.
  10. Feed the dough through the spout and plunge it down.
  11. Watch the beuatiful pasta shoot out!.
  12. Boil for five minutes.
  13. Drain out water.
  14. Serve with tomato sauce or however you like to eat pasta enjoy!.
