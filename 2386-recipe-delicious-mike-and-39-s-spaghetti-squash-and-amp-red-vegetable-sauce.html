Mike's Spaghetti Squash & Red Vegetable Sauce instructions

  1. Here's the bulk of what you'll need. Rough chop all vegetables..
  2. Saute your vegetables in olive oil for 5 minutes. Add everything to your pot - except for squash - and simmer for 30 minutes..
  3. Use a quality deep red wine..
  4. Season squash and spray with olive oil..
  5. Place in a 350° oven for 60 minutes..
  6. Shread squash with a fork and place in bowls..
  7. Toast your garlic bread until crispy..
  8. Serve sauce over noodles with garlic bread and a quality red wine. Garnish with shredded parmesan and fresh parsley. Enjoy!.
