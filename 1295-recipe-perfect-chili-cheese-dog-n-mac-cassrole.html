Chili cheese dog n mac cassrole step by step

  1. Fix mac n cheese as box says then put in a baking dish add a layer of shredded cheese.
  2. Pour hot dogs on top of mac n shredded cheese them pour chili on top sprinkle more shredded cheese.
  3. Mix up cornbread mix and pour on top. Bake at 350° till cornbread is done top with cheese and enjoy..
