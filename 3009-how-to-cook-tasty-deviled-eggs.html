Deviled Eggs instructions

  1. Use eggs that are 2 weeks old.
  2. rest eggs over night on side.
  3. remove eggs from fridge 30 min before cooking.
  4. place eggs in pot.
  5. fill with cold water to cover by 1 inch.
  6. add 1 tsp salt.
  7. cover pot put on high burner.
  8. as soon as u get big bubbles and steam move to cold burner.
  9. add 1 tbsp of vinegar.
  10. let set 30 min to finish cooking.
  11. cool in a bowl of ice water 20 min.
  12. gently crack egg all over on counter place back in water for 10 min.
  13. remove shell and membrane in running water.
  14. cut eggs and pull out yolks with a spoon.
  15. mash yolks with a fork.
  16. sprinkle with paprika.
