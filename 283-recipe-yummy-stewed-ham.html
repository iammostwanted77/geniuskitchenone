Stewed Ham.

Ingredients of Stewed Ham

  1. Prepare of Stew.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of dijon mustard.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of dark brown sugar.
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup of soya sauce.
  5. It's 1/2 stick of butter.
  6. It's 1/2 teaspoon of ground white pepper.
  7. It's 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  8. It's 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar.
  9. Prepare 1 of medium green bell pepper.
  10. You need 1 of shallot.
  11. It's 1/2 cup of watermelon.
  12. Prepare of Ham.
  13. It's 2 pounds of smoked ham.
  14. You need of Pasta.
  15. It's 4 ounces of lasagna pasta.
  16. It's As needed of water to boil pasta.
  17. It's 1 tablespoons of salt for boiling pasta.
  18. It's 1 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil to boil pasta.
  19. Prepare 1/2 stick of butter to fry pasta.
  20. It's 1 tablespoon of soya sauce.
  21. You need 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar.

Stewed Ham step by step

  1. Boil the pasta as instructed on the package. Drain and cool in the freezer or fridge..
  2. Slice the watermelon, bell pepper and shallot. Mix the stew ingredients together and add to a pan. Turn on the heat..
  3. Slice the ham. When the stew mixture is heated, add the ham slices. Coat the entire surface of each piece of ham..
  4. Turn as needed and stew in the mixture for 15 minutes. Cover and remove from heat. Let rest 12 minutes before serving..
  5. Melt the tablespoon soya sauce and butter in the pan. As the ham rest, cut the pasta in wide strips and fry in vinegar, butter/soya sauce mixture..
  6. Let pasta absorb some of the flavors. The stewed ham is done. Serve, together. I hope you enjoy!!!.
