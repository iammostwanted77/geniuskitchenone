Brown rice phirni instructions

  1. Soak rice for atleast 1 hr..
  2. Pat dry the rice using paper towel..
  3. Now using blender make a coarse powder..
  4. Now 1 cup milk mix with rice and keep aside for 10-15 mins..
  5. In a heavy bottom vessels take rest of milk and start boiling. When it starts boil add rice and low the flame. Cook rice in low flame for 15-20 mins untill rice become soft and well blended with milk..
  6. When rice done add mongo puree and honey. Cook another 4-5 mins in low flame. If you want more sweets then add 2 tsp melted jaggery in this time..
  7. Add saffron and off the flame..
  8. Closed the lid for 2-3 mins. It makes nice colour for saffron..
  9. Now pour to earthen pot or any other pot. Keep for refrigerator for atleast 1-2 hours..
  10. Now garnish with your favourite ingredients and served chilled..
