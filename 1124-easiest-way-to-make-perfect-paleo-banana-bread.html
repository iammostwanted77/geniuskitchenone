Ingredients of Paleo Banana Bread

  1. It's 3 of Bananas.
  2. It's 3 of Eggs.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of Vanilla extract.
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp of Honey.
  5. It's 1/4 cup of Extra virgin coconut oil.
  6. Prepare 2 cup of Blanched almond flour.
  7. You need 1 tsp of Celtic sea salt.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of Baking soda.

Paleo Banana Bread instructions

  1. 1. Place bananas, eggs, vanilla, honey and shortening in a food processor. 2. Pulse ingredients together. 3. Pulse in almond flour, salt and baking soda. 4. Scoop batter into a greased 7.5x3.5 loaf pan. 5. Bake at 350°F for 35 min. 6. Remove from oven and allow to cool before cutting..
