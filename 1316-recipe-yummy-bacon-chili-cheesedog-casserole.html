Bacon Chili Cheesedog Casserole instructions

  1. Slice the stew meats, onion, hotdogs, and bacon. Render the fat..
  2. When the fat is rendered add the onions and sauté till slightly caramelized. Add the ground beef, stewmeats, and then add the hotdogs when the other meats are browned. Simmer 15 minutes add ketchup and mustard..
  3. Stir well let simmer 15 minutes more..
  4. Mix the topping ingredients. Adding the cheese last.
  5. Pour on top of the casserole. Put into the oven for 30-40 minutes. Serve I hope you enjoy. You can add the cayenne pepper here..
