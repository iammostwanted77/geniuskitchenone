Fruit filled crepe-ala-pancake lol instructions

  1. Make pancake recipe make sure it's a little runny.
  2. Next take frozen fruit put in microwave safe bowl microwave for few minutes then take out add sugar (filling sugar) then microwave for few more minutes until warm (microwaves temp may vary).
  3. Then oil pan and heat til a drop of water sizzles then add pancake mix.
  4. Cook til the pancake top is bubbles then add couple tablespoons of sugar fruit mixture to pancake top then flip in half as if ur making an omlete.
  5. Cook for a few minutes flip onto plate making sure the pancake stays folded in half with fruit Inside of it.
  6. Then take little bit of fruit mixture juice and pour over it.
  7. Enjoy! If you can fix recipe or add anything feel free to share it with me thank you.
