Ingredients of Cheesy Green Bean Casserole

  1. You need 3 cans of cut green beans.
  2. Prepare 1 can (10 oz) of cream of mushroom soup.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese.
  4. Prepare 1 bag of cheddar french fried onions.

Cheesy Green Bean Casserole instructions

  1. Open green beans and pour into pot with the juices, then bring to a boil. Once boiling, strain and pour into baking dish..
  2. Pour cream of mushroom soup over green beans and then 3/4 of the cheese. Mix together in baking dish..
  3. Top casserole with fried onions and the rest of the cheese..
  4. Bake at 350°F for 30 minutes or until brown on top and cheese is melted..
