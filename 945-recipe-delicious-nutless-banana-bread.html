Ingredients of Nutless Banana Bread

  1. You need 3 of bananas.
  2. It's 1/3 cup of melted butter.
  3. Prepare 1 cup of sugar.
  4. It's 1 of beaten egg.
  5. You need 1 tsp of vanilla.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of baking soda.
  7. You need 1 pinch of of salt.
  8. It's 1 1/2 cup of flour.

Nutless Banana Bread step by step

  1. preheat oven to 350°.
  2. smash bananas and mix with butter.
  3. mix in sugar, egg and vanilla.
  4. sprinkle salt and baking soda over mixture, then stir.
  5. add flour. mix well.
  6. if mixture is too thick of stiff, add a splash of milk and stir.
  7. pour into a grease loaf pan..
  8. bake for 1 hour. remove from oven. let cool.
  9. Enjoy!.
