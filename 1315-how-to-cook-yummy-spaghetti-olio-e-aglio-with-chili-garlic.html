Spaghetti Olio E Aglio With Chili Garlic instructions

  1. Bring a lightly salted pot of water then add in spaghetti for 8 to 10 minutes then drain.
  2. With oil sauté the (1/2)slice garlic till it’s lightly brown then set aside as garnish, then (1/2) sauté the pounded garlic with a dash of salt for half a minute, and not brown.
  3. Add in the spaghetti and fresh coriander with a dash of salt and mix well.
  4. Add in 1 tsp garlic Chili or dried Chili flake and mix well and enjoy.
  5. Remake Today, Linguine Olio E Aglio.
