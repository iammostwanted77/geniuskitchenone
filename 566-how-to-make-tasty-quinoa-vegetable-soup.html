How to Make Tasty Quinoa vegetable soup

Delicious, fresh and tasty.

Quinoa vegetable soup.

You can have Quinoa vegetable soup using 17 ingredients and 13 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Quinoa vegetable soup

  1. It's 1/2 cup of Quinoa.
  2. It's 1/2 cup of Carrot.
  3. It's 1/2 cup of a mixture of green peas & sweet Corn.
  4. You need 1/3 cup of chopped Beetroot.
  5. Prepare 1/3 cup of chopped Potato.
  6. You need 2 of medium sized roughly chopped Tomatoes.
  7. You need 2 of medium sized chopped Onion.
  8. Prepare 1 of small chopped Celery.
  9. Prepare 4 of Garlic cloves crushed.
  10. It's 1/2 teaspoon of Kashmiri red Chilli powder.
  11. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon of Thyme.
  12. You need 1 of Bay leaf.
  13. Prepare 1 tablespoon of Olive oil.
  14. Prepare 1/2 of Lemon.
  15. You need to taste of Salt.
  16. Prepare as needed of parsley or any other herb.
  17. Prepare 4 teaspoons of grated Cheese to garnish.

Quinoa vegetable soup step by step

  1. Clean and rinse quinoa well and set aside..
  2. Chop all the vegetables..
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a heavy-bottomed pan and add in chopped onion, crushed garlic and chopped celery..
  4. Saute for a couple of minutes and then add in all the vegetables and saute for few more minutes..
  5. Add in chilli powder,thyme, bay leaf and salt..
  6. Roughly chop tomatoes and grind to make a paste..
  7. Add in this tomato paste to the pan and stir well to mix..
  8. Also, add in quinoa and 5 cups of hot water..
  9. When the mixture comes to a boil, cover and simmers for about 45 minutes..
  10. After 45 minutes the soup will be almost ready. Before removing add in the juice of half of a lemon and parsley..
  11. Give the last stir and remove from heat..
  12. Transfer to individual bowls, garnish with some grated cheese and serve immediately..
