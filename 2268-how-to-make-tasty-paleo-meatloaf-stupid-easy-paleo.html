  2. Take all other meat ingredients and mix by hand, adding onion & red pepper after other ingredients have been mixed.
  3. Set oven to 400? For loaf pan, bake for 35-45 minutes For muffin pan, bake for 25-30 minutes.
  4. Mix sauce ingredients.
