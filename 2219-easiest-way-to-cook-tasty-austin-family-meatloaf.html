Ingredients of Austin Family Meatloaf

  1. You need of Meatloaf.
  2. Prepare 4 lb of Hamburger Meat (I used 80/20).
  3. Prepare 2 of Eggs.
  4. You need 1 of medium onion, chopped.
  5. You need 1/2 box of Ritz Crackers (can substitute saltines or toast).
  6. Prepare 1 packages of Dry Italian or Ranch Dressing Mix.
  7. It's of Salt.
  8. Prepare of Pepper.
  9. It's of Garlic Salt.
  10. It's 12 oz of Can of Tomato Paste.
  11. It's 1 cup of Ketchup.

Austin Family Meatloaf step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°.
  2. Crumble raw hamburger into a large bowl..
  3. Crumble Ritz Crackers (Saltines or toast) into the same bowl with the hamburger..
  4. In same bowl add eggs, onion, salt, pepper, garlic salt & dressing mix..
  5. Add approx 3/4 of your tomato paste to the mixture..
  6. Mix everything together by hand (fun right?) until all blended together. :-).
  7. Spread in a sprayed or greased 9 X 13 pan and bake for 35 minutes..
  8. While meatloaf is baking take your remaining tomato paste and your ketchup and mix together..
  9. After 35 minutes in the oven spread the ketchup mixture on top of meatloaf and bake an additional 10 minutes..
  10. Enjoy!!! :-D.
