Ingredients of Devil's deviled eggs

  1. It's 1 dozen of eggs.
  2. You need 4 tbsp of miracle whip.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of Sriracha hot chili sause.
  4. You need 2 tsp of spicy mustard (regular for mild).
  5. Prepare 2 tsp of dill relish.
  6. Prepare 2 tsp of sour cream.
  7. You need 1 tsp of horseradish.
  8. You need dash of salt.
  9. It's dash of ground black pepper.
  10. You need dash of paprika.

Devil's deviled eggs step by step

  1. Place eggs in a pot with cold water, bring to a boil. Add salt and leave for about 10 - 15 minutes..
  2. Remove from heat and run cold water over eggs in the sink to remove hot water. Then let stand for about 5 min or until eggs are cool.
  3. Peel off the shells and slice lengthwise in half. Remove the yoke and place in mixing bowl, set egg whites on a platter to the side..
  4. Add miracle whip, Sriracha hot chili sause, mustard, dill relish, sour cream, horseradish and a dash of salt&pepper to the mixing bowl with the yokes. Mix well for a few minutes (add a little milk if you like it creamy).
  5. With a spoon place a dollop of the mixture into the egg whites. A dash of paprika on top and your done!.
