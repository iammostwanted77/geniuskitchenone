Ingredients of Turkey 2x meatloaf

  1. It's 1/3 lb of ground beef.
  2. Prepare 1/3 lb of ground turkey.
  3. Prepare 2 of turkey sausage (spicy or not).
  4. You need 1 large of eggs.
  5. Prepare 2/3 cup of Italian breadcrumbs.
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup of your favorite BBQ sauce.
  7. It's 2 tbsp of minced garlic.

Turkey 2x meatloaf step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Remove sausage from casings.
  3. Mash up all that meat and try to disperse the sausage & minced garlic evenly.
  4. Crack your egg and mix thoroughly.
  5. Spread your BBQ sauce on the top to glaze.
  6. Bake for 1 hour.
