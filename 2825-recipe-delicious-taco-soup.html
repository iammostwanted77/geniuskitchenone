Ingredients of Taco Soup

  1. Prepare 1 pound of ground beef.
  2. It's 1 of onion, sliced.
  3. Prepare 1 of green pepper, sliced.
  4. You need of Fresh minced garlic (about 6 of them, minced) I used xtra.
  5. It's 1 can (28 oz.) of crushed tomatoes.
  6. Prepare 1 can of black beans (undrained).
  7. Prepare 1 cup of corn.
  8. You need 1 teaspoon of black pepper.
  9. It's 1 cup of water.
  10. You need 3 tablespoons of taco seasoning (or you can use 1 pack of taco seasoning).

Taco Soup instructions

  1. Brown ground beef in a pan....Add sliced onion and green pepper to meat in pan....
  2. Add mined garlic to browned meat, onions and green pepper....cook for about 2 minutes.....
  3. Add black pepper and taco seasoning to pan, then add can of black beans and the water....
  4. Add the corn and the can of crushed tomatoes to pan, mix well......
  5. Bring all to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cook for 45 minutes, covered,stirring occasionally....
