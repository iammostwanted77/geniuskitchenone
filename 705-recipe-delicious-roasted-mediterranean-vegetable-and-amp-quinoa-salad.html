Roasted Mediterranean Vegetable & Quinoa Salad instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 390.
  2. Mix the vegetables with the chili paste and garlic to it is well coated..
  3. Roast for 20 - 30 minutes until it is slightly brown..
  4. Cook the quinoa by adding it to a sauce pan with vegetable stock. Cook on a simmer for approximate 15 - 20 minutes until the liquid is absorbed..
  5. Stir the onions and tomato puree into the quinoa..
  6. Mix in the roasted vegetables..
