KETO - Meatloaf instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 360 degrees..
  2. Combine all meats, seasonings, eggs, and pork rinds and 2 tbsp Ketchup, hand mix to combine thoroughly..
  3. Place in loaf pan and brush top of loaf with remaining ketchup and Worcestershire sauce..
  4. Bake for 60 minutes, removing every 20 to brush more ketchup and Worcestershire sauce..
  5. Remove, and let sit for 5 minutes..
  6. Bon appetit.
