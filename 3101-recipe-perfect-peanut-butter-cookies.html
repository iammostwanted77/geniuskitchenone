Ingredients of Peanut butter cookies

  1. Prepare 200 g of peanut butter.
  2. It's 60 g of roasted peanut, crush it.
  3. You need 150 g of caster sugar.
  4. Prepare 375 g of plain flour.
  5. You need 200 ml of vegetable oil.
  6. Prepare 1 1/4 tsp of salt.
  7. It's 2 of egg yolks.
  8. You need 45 of roasted cashew nuts.

Peanut butter cookies instructions

  1. Mix all the ingredients, except for egg yolks and cashew nuts.
  2. Take one table spoon of the mix, make it a round, and push the middle a little bit, and put one cashew on it. Repeat the step untill all the mix used. (Brush the egg yolks to the cookies before put on the cashew).
  3. Bake in the preheated oven 350°F for 20-25 minutes.
