Ingredients of Perfect Peanut Butter Cookies

  1. It's 1/2 cup of Butter.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of Brown Sugar.
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup of White Granulated Sugar.
  4. You need 1/2 cup of Peanut Butter.
  5. It's 1 1/2 cup of Flour.
  6. You need 1 of Egg.
  7. You need 3/4 tsp of Baking Soda.
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp of Baking Powder.
  9. Prepare 1 packages of Hershey's Kisses.
  10. It's 1 of Additional Sugar.

Perfect Peanut Butter Cookies instructions

  1. Preheat Oven to 375°F Fahrenheit. In a large bowl, mix together the 1/2 cup sugar, brown sugar, egg, butter and peanut butter. Use either an electric mixer on medium or a a wooden spoon by hand. Be sure to mix well to a creamy consistency. Gradually stir in flour, baking soda and baking powder until a soft dough forms..
  2. Shape dough into 1 inch balls and roll in additional sugar. Place dough 2 inches apart on UNGREASED cookie sheets..
  3. Bake 8 to 10 minutes until edges of dough are lightly golden brown. Immediately place one Hershey's Kiss In the center of each cookie and move cookies to a cooling rack..
