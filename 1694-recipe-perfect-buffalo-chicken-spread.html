Buffalo chicken spread step by step

  1. Mix cream cheese & ranch packet together. Spread onto a cookie sheet..
  2. Put shredded chicken on top of cream cheese..
  3. Drizzle buffalo sauce over chicken..
  4. Top with shredded cheddar cheese..
  5. Bake at 350' for about 10 mins or until cheese is melted..
  6. Eat with chips or spread on french bread. Enjoy!.
