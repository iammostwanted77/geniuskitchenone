Honey Baked Ham Sandwich Lunchbox instructions

  1. Cut bread into halves. Cut ham, lettuce and cheese into slices of similar size to cut bread..
  2. Roll ham, lettuce and cheese in a slice of cut bread and hold in place with a toothpick..
  3. Wash grapes and cherry tomatoes..
  4. Portion nuts and dried fruit in cupcake cup..
  5. Arrange everything to fit together in a lunchbox.
  6. Have a good lunch!!.
