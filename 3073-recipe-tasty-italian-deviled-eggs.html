Italian deviled eggs step by step

  1. In a saucepan boil egg by adding water and salt for 12 - 15 minutes..
  2. Peel the shell of the egg once they are hard boiled..
  3. Cut the boiled eggs into half and scoop out the yolks..
  4. In yolks add Mayonnaise, pepper powder, red chilli powder, lemon juice and salt and mash this mixture with fork. Now we will get creamy and smooth stuffing..
  5. Fill the stuffing in a plastic piping bag making sure there are no air pockets and fit it with a nozzle..
  6. Now fill this stuffing in the half empty egg carefully..
  7. Finally, garnish with some oregano and mint leaves. Creamy delicious Italian deviled eggs are ready to be served!.
