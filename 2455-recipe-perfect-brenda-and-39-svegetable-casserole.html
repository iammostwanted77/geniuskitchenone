Brenda'sVegetable casserole instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°.
  2. Spray 2 quart casserole dish with Pam.
  3. Spread mixed vegetables in dish.
  4. Saute onion & garlic in small amount of olive oil till soft..
  5. Spread garlic onion mixture over vegetables..
  6. Salt & pepper to taste.
  7. Spread sauce over top, cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes..
  8. Uncover and bake for another 15 minutes or until vegetables are fork tender..
  9. Sprinkle with cheese and leave in oven turned off until cheese is lightly browned..
