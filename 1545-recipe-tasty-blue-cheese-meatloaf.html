Blue Cheese Meatloaf.

You can cook Blue Cheese Meatloaf using 10 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Blue Cheese Meatloaf

  1. It's 1 lb of ground turkey.
  2. It's 1/4 cup of diced onions.
  3. It's 1/4 cup of diced pepper.
  4. It's 1 tbsp of olive oil.
  5. You need 1/2 cup of milk.
  6. It's 1/4 cup of ketchup.
  7. You need 1/4 cup of barbecue sauce.
  8. It's 2 of eggs.
  9. Prepare 4 tbsp of crumble blue cheese.
  10. Prepare 1 cup of mixer of bread crumbs and panko.

Blue Cheese Meatloaf step by step

  1. Saute onions and peppers in olive oil..
  2. In a bowl mix together ground turkey, milk, ketchup, barbecue sauce, eggs and blue cheese. Add the peppers and onions. Mix in the bread crumbs and panko..
  3. Once blended together, put into a loaf pan. Top with barbecue sauce..
  4. Set oven at 400°F and cook for 1 hour 15 minutes..
