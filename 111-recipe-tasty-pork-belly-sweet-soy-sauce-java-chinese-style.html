Ingredients of Pork belly sweet soy sauce ( Java-Chinese style)

  1. Prepare 4 clove of garlic cloves.
  2. Prepare 2 oz of pork belly.
  3. You need 1 cup of soy sauce.
  4. Prepare 1 3/4 cup of sweet soy sauce.
  5. You need 2 tbsp of tong cay ( Chinese salted vegetable optional).
  6. You need 4 tbsp of rice wine.
  7. You need 3 tbsp of vegetable oil.
  8. It's 3 of boiled eggs.
  9. Prepare of simple sweet soy sauce (mix).
  10. Prepare 3/4 cup of brown sugar.
  11. Prepare 1 cup of dark soy sauce.

Pork belly sweet soy sauce ( Java-Chinese style) step by step

  1. Marinade pork with soy sauce and sweet soy sauce mixture for about 30 minutes.
  2. Dice garlic cloves and stir fry until Brown..
  3. Add in tong cay.
  4. Add marinated pork into the pan, and add in some rice wine.
  5. Add boiled eggs, abd 3 cups of water, Put the lid on. And cook for about 3 hours..
