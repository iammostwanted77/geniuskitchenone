Currin's Spaghetti Squash Incident instructions

  1. Place whole spaghetti squash in microwave and cook for one minute. Remove and cut in half lengthwise. Remove all seeds and membranes..
  2. Place squash cut side down in a 13x9 casserole dish. Add 1 1/2 Cups water. Bake 30 minutes at 350°F Fahrenheit..
  3. Meanwhile, sauté onion for 5 minutes. Add garlic and sauté another minute. Transfer onion mix into a pot..
  4. Add all seasonings, salt and pepper. Stir. Add the tomato sauces. Mix..
  5. Bring to a boil, then lower heat to low or medium low. Simmer 30 minutes, stirring occasionally..
  6. Test squash tenderness with a fork. Should be al dente. Once done, remove from casserole. With fork, scrape squash to create "spaghetti strands.".
  7. Serve in large bowls. Top with sauce..
