You can have Zesty Onion-Crimini Quinoa using 10 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Zesty Onion-Crimini Quinoa

  1. It's 2 cup of water.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of uncooked quinoa.
  3. It's 4 tbsp of olive oil.
  4. It's 1 large of minced yellow onion.
  5. You need 1/4 lb of sliced crimini mushrooms.
  6. You need 1 of juice of half a lemon.
  7. You need 1 of zest of 1 lemon.
  8. It's 1/4 tsp of chicken or beef flavoring/seasoning.
  9. You need 1 dash of of fresh ground black pepper to taste.
  10. It's 1/3 cup of chopped flat leaf parsley.

Zesty Onion-Crimini Quinoa step by step

  1. Soak quinoa in water for 10 minutes, rinse and drain well..
  2. Place quinoa + 2 cups of water in a small pot. Cook on Medium heat covered. Let boil, turn stove to Low, then let it simmer for 20 mins. (Turn stove off. Do NOT lift lid)..
  3. While quinoa is simmering, heat pan on stove on Medium heat..
  4. Add onions & crimini to pan and allow both to caramelize..
  5. Stir in chicken /beef flavor and ground black pepper..
  6. Turn stove off..
  7. Add lemon juice & zest to pan. Mix well then add warm quinoa and chopped parsley. (Fluff or fold gently until all ingredients are evenly incorporated.).
  8. Serve warm. (Note: If you have chicken/beef broth, you may omit the 2 cups of water and chicken/beef flavor. Instead, cook the quinoa in 2 cups of broth.).
