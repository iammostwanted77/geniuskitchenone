Ingredients of Epic Meatloaf

  1. It's 1 1/2 lb of ground beef.
  2. You need 1 of egg.
  3. You need 1 cup of Italian style bread crumbs.
  4. Prepare 1 cup of sauteed onions.
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup of milk.
  6. It's 1/2 cup of ketchup.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of Worcestershire Sauce.
  8. It's 1 dash of salt and pepper.
  9. It's 1 clove of garlic, chopped.
  10. It's 2 tbsp of light brown sugar.
  11. Prepare 1 dash of yellow mustard.
  12. Prepare 1 dash of cider vinegar.

Epic Meatloaf step by step

  1. Saute onions and garlic together..
  2. Combine beef, bread crumbs, onions, garlic, ketchup, Worcestershire, milk, egg and salt/pepper by kneading together in a bowl..
  3. Place mixture in loaf pan.
  4. Glaze- combine ketchup, brown sugar, vinegar, and mustard to taste for a BBQ like glaze. Put glaze over loaf, and bake at 350 for apx an hour..
