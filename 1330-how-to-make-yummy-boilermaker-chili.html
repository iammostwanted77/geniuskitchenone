Boilermaker chili instructions

  1. Brown ground beef and sausage in 5 quart pot on medium-high heat. Drain excess grease..
  2. Mix all additional ingredients in pot and bring to a boil..
  3. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 2 hours. Taste and adjust salt, pepper and chili powder if needed. The longer it simmers and stands the better the taste..
  4. Allow chili to cool and top with cheddar cheese..
