Ingredients of Italian Meatloaf

  1. It's of ingredients:.
  2. You need 2 tbsp of olive oil, extra virgin.
  3. You need 1 small of red bell pepper, diced.
  4. You need 1 small of white onion, diced.
  5. It's 3 clove of of garlic, minced.
  6. It's 2 lb of meatloaf mix (ground beef and pork).
  7. You need 2 of eggs.
  8. You need 1 cup of Italian seasoned bread crumbs.
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese.
  10. You need 1 tbsp of worcestershire sauce.
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp of balsamic vinegar.
  12. It's 2 tbsp of chopped basil.
  13. You need 1 tbsp of chopped parsley.
  14. Prepare 2 tsp of salt.
  15. It's 1/2 tsp of black pepper.
  16. You need 1/2 cup of marinera sauce.
  17. You need 1/2 cup of ketchup.

Italian Meatloaf instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F..
  2. Heat olive oil in skillet, over low-medium heat. Add pepper, onion, and garlic..
  3. Saute until translucent, then remove from heat..
  4. Combine all other ingredients (hands work the best, just make sure their clean!) EXCEPT FOR THE MARINERA AND KETCHUP!.
  5. Add cooled sauteed veggies.
  6. Mix well!!.
  7. Form into loaf on a baking pan, the one I used is a 11x17. With plenty of extra space.
  8. Mix together both marinera sauce and ketchup, pour about half over top of loaf. Then place in oven to bake for about 50-60 minutes, or until center reaches 160°F..
  9. Finished product! I served this with a side of Oven Baked Parsley Red Potatoes! https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/355410-oven-baked-parsley-red-potatoes.
