Bacon Wrapped Cheesesteak Meatloaf instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 360.
  2. Saute peppers and onions until soft.
  3. Mix beef with stuffing mix, water, egg, cayenne pepper, salt, black pepper and oregano. Spread out into even rectangle on tin foil..
  4. Lay cheese slices in middle topped with peppers and onions..
  5. Gently roll meat into sausage shape..
  6. Lay strips of bacon and roll meat mixture..
  7. Bake covered for 30 minutes, uncover and bake 40 minutes longer..
