Ingredients of Crustless Green Bean Casserole

  1. Prepare 4 cans of green beans.
  2. You need 1/4 of red onion; julienne.
  3. Prepare 1/3 of yellow bell pepper; julienne.
  4. You need 6 of garlic cloves; creamed.
  5. You need 1 can of cream of mushroom soup.
  6. It's 1 can of creamed corn.
  7. Prepare 1 stick of cheddar; cubed.
  8. You need 1 T of crushed red pepper flakes.
