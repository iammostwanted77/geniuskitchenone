smoky green bean casserole instructions

  1. blanch beans 4-6 min. cook bacon until crisp, remove from skillet..
  2. cook onion in 2 T. drippings until tender, add flour. stir in milk & cook 3 min. until thickened. add cheese & stir until melted..
  3. stir in beans & bacon & transfer to 1 & 1/2 qt. casserole. top with cracker crumbs & bake at 350 for 25 min..
