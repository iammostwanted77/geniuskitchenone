Homestyle Deviled Eggs step by step

  1. Hardboil 6 Eggs.
  2. Peel and slice the hardboiled eggs in half.
  3. Scoop out the yellow egg yoke and place them into a small mixing bowl.
  4. Pour in the ground mustard, pickle relish, and mayo into that small mixing bowl with the yoke, stir it until its smooth.
  5. Spoon it out of the mixing bowl and into the egg half's, and store in the fridge for 20 mins. Then serve and enjoy!.
