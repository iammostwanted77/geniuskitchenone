Cheesy Chicken Green Bean Casserole.

You can have Cheesy Chicken Green Bean Casserole using 12 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Cheesy Chicken Green Bean Casserole

  1. Prepare 16 oz of green beans.
  2. It's 10 oz of cream of onion.
  3. You need 5 oz of chicken breast.
  4. It's 1/2 cup of shredded cheese.
  5. It's 1/2 cup of fried onions.
  6. It's 1/4 cup of tomato.
  7. Prepare 1/8 cup of roasted peppers.
  8. You need 1 of portabella mushroom.
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp of dijon mustard.
  10. It's 1/2 tbsp of hot sauce.
  11. Prepare 1/2 tbsp of garlic salt.
  12. It's 1/2 tsp of cayenne pepper.

Cheesy Chicken Green Bean Casserole step by step

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F..
  2. Cut chicken breast into 1 - inch cubes. Place in baggie, add garlic salt and cayenne pepper. Shake to evenly distribute..
  3. Wash tomato and mushroom, dice..
  4. Combine green beans, cream of onion, mustard, hot sauce, tomato, mushroom, cheese, chicken, and roasted peppers in mixing bowl. Stir together..
  5. Spread evenly in small baking pan, and sprinkle fried onions on top..
  6. Cover with aluminum foil, bake for 35-40 minutes..
