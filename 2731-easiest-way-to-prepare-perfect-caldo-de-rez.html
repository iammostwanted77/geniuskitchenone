Ingredients of Caldo de rez

  1. It's 3 lbs of beef roast fat and bone included cut up in chunks.
  2. Prepare 3 cups of fresh green beans.
  3. Prepare to taste of Salt.
  4. You need 4 of fresh corn on the cob cut into 1/3's.
  5. It's 1 bunch of cilantro.
  6. It's 1 of large onion half finely chopped and half chopped chunky.
  7. Prepare of Water.
  8. Prepare of Serrano chiles chopped up.

Caldo de rez step by step

  1. In large pot add water and beef, beef bone and beef fat. Cook till fork tender..
  2. Add chunky cut onion, cilantro, green beans, corn and salt to taste. Continue cooking until beef is pull apart tender..
  3. Serve in large bowl with chopped onion, chopped serrano chiles and lime juice..
