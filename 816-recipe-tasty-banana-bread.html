Ingredients of Banana bread

  1. Prepare 2 of bananas.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of flour(I used cornflour and wholewheat flour 50:50).
  3. It's 2 tbsp of sugarless(dutch)cocoa powder.
  4. It's 2 tsp of cinnamon.
  5. You need 1/2 cup of nuts(I used only walnut).
  6. You need 1 tsp of oil(olive/coconut or any other type of oil).
  7. It's 1/2 cup of water.
  8. You need 1 tsp of vanilla essence.
  9. You need 1/2 packet of baking powder.
  10. Prepare 1 pinch of salt.
  11. It's 2 tbsp of sweetener(I used eritrit).

Banana bread instructions

  1. Smash the bananas with a fork in a large bowl..
  2. Then add all the other ingredients to the banana and mix them well..
  3. Take this mix into a tray which is covered with baking paper and a bit of butter or oil. Bake it for about 30 minutes at 200°C..
