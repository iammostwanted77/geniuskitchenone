Ingredients of Festive green bean casserole

  1. Prepare 2 can of cream of mushroom soup.
  2. It's 24 oz of fresh green beans.
  3. You need of 2 diced peppers ( i used red and orange).
  4. It's 3/4 cup of milk ( i used 2%).
  5. You need dash of black pepper.
  6. It's pinch of granulated sugar.
  7. It's 1 1/2 can of French's fried onions.
