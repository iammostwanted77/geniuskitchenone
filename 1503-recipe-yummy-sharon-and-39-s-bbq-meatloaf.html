Sharon's BBQ meatloaf instructions

  1. Pre heat oven to 350°f.
  2. In a large bowl mix all ingredients EXCEPT for bbq sauce.
  3. Once meatloaf mixture is mixed remove from bowl and place in baking dish.
  4. Form a loaf with the meatloaf mixture.
  5. Bake in oven UNCOVERED for an hour.
  6. Remove from oven; spread bbq sauce on top of meatloaf until evenly coated.
  7. Place meatloaf back in oven for additonal 30 mins.
  8. Remove from oven and ENJOY!!.
