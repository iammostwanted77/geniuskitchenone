French's original green bean casserole step by step

  1. preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. mix soup and milk..
  3. add black pepper, beans, and 2/3c onions.
  4. bake @350for 30 mins or until hot.
  5. stir and then add remaining onions and replace back into oven for an additional 5 mins..
