Ingredients of Chinese red beans (adzuki beans)

  1. You need 1 lb of adzuki beans.
  2. You need 1 of water to cover and soak in a container.
  3. Prepare 3 quart of water.
  4. You need 4 oz of margarine, butter or ghee.
  5. You need 2 tbsp of granulated garlic powder.
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of ground black pepper.
  7. It's 2 tsp of salt.
  8. Prepare 1 small of onion chopped.
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp of jalapeño juice from a jar of sliced jalapeños.
