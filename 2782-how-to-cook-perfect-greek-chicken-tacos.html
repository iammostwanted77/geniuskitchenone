Ingredients of Greek chicken tacos

  1. It's 1 cup of plain greek yogurt.
  2. Prepare 1/2 of cucumber diced.
  3. It's 1/2 of red onion diced.
  4. Prepare 1 clove of garlic finely chopped.
  5. It's 1 of tomato diced.
  6. It's 1 of lemon halved.
  7. Prepare of Chicken breasts.
  8. Prepare of Feta cheese.
  9. Prepare of Flour tortillas.

Greek chicken tacos step by step

  1. Prepare tsatziki sauce. Mix yogurt, cucumber, red onion, garlic, squeeze of half a lemon, salt and pepper. Refrigerate..
  2. Season chicken with salt, pepper, oregano and squeeze of lemon. Grill is best but pan sear if needed. Once cooked slice pretty thinly or chunk..
  3. Grill tortillas or cook over gas stove preferably. Otherwise you can microwave to warm if desperate but cooking ove an open flame to get a tiny crispy is best..
  4. Sauté or grill the other half of the sliced red onion..
  5. Assemble taco. Sauce first, chicken, tomato and feta cheese. Enjoy!!.
