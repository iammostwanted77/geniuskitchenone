Ingredients of Kabli pulao

  1. It's 1 kg of soaked rice.
  2. It's Half of kg grated carrots.
  3. It's 1 cup of kishmish (mew).
  4. Prepare of Oil as required.
  5. You need 2 of medium sized chopped onions.
  6. You need 1 of ts garlic and Ginger paste.
  7. Prepare 1 kg of beef.
  8. Prepare of Water as required.
  9. It's to taste of Salt.
  10. It's 1 of ts black pepper.
  11. It's 1 of ts garam masala powder.
  12. Prepare 1 of ts sugar.

Kabli pulao instructions

  1. Peel and Cutt carrots into thin strips.
  2. Now heat oil in pan, add half ts sugar and shallow fry carrots for 2-3 minutes, then remove carrots from oil and set aside.
  3. Now shallow fry kishmish in the same oil in which we fried carrots, add half ts sugar as well and mix, set aside.
  4. Now heat oil in a pan, add onions, when its colors changes to brown,add beef fry beef for 10 minute, add spices and mix now add 2 glass of water and cook it untill beef become soft.
  5. Now boil rice in salt water.
  6. Now its time to assemble, put beef mixture on boiled rice.
  7. Then add fried carrots and kishmish, cover the pan for 10 minutes on low flame.
  8. Mix, and serve.
