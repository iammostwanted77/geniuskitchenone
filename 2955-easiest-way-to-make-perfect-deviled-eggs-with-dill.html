  3. Prepare 2 tbsp of mayo.

Deviled Eggs with Dill instructions

  1. broil eggs in water until hard.
  2. cut hard broiled eggs in halves longways.
  3. gently spoon the yolks in to a bowl without breaking the egg.
  4. mix all ingredients until smooth and creamy.
  5. spoon mixture in to egg halves.
  6. sprinkle dill on top.
  7. served chilled.
