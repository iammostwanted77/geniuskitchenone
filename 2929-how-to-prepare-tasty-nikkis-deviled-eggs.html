nikkis deviled eggs instructions

  1. Boil eggs 20-30 min. After done you can let them cool down .Or if in a hurry , put the eggs in a bowl of cool water to cool down faster ..
  2. After cooled down , peel the shells off . Carefully so the egg its self won't peel off. After done slice eggs in half (whichever way you prefer)..
  3. Put yolk into bowl and with a fork , mash the yolk until it looks like its fine pieces (kind of like powder but thicker)..
  4. Add the mayo , mustard Tabasco sauce . Mix it all together ..
  5. If adding pickles , you can dice them up into little pieces . (you can add relish or nothing at all. Depending on your taste .) After you're done cutting add them in the mixture and mix them . Also add the pinch of sugar (depending how much you want & to your taste ).
  6. Mix it all together . You can add the mixture into a zip lock baggie twist it up and cut the corner of the bag and use it to squeeze out the mixture in a neat way and not messy. or you can use a spoon ..
  7. (OPTIONAL) after done . You can sprinkle paprika . I lightly add Tabasco sauce ..
  8. (if you like it sweet I suggest you use Kraft's miracle whip mayo. If you don't have any use a little more sugar . Tangy a little more mustard. Want it with a kick ? add more Tabasco sauce or maybe even add a slice of jalepeno on the top. ENJOY..
