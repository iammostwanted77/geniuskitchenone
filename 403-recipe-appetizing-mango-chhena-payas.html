Ingredients of Mango chhena payas

  1. It's 1 litre of full fat milk, reduce to half after boiling.
  2. It's 2 of medium mango, chopped.
  3. Prepare 20-22 of saffron strands.
  4. It's 2 tsp of sugar.
  5. It's 1 pinch of salt.
  6. You need 1 tsp of black cardamom powder.
  7. Prepare of Some cashews, almonds, chopped to garnish.
  8. You need of Some raisins, also to garnish.

Mango chhena payas step by step

  1. Wash, peel off the mangoes and chop them. Make purée in grinder..
  2. Boil the already reduced and thickened milk, on low to medium heat..
  3. Add saffron strands, sugar and the mango puree to the boiling milk..
  4. Lower the flame and stir the mixture of milk and mango puree on light hand..
  5. You can notice the cuddling of milk leading to a nice amalgamation of chhena and mango purée, leading to granular form, indicating chhena (cheese) formation..
  6. Cook for two minutes further switch off the flame. Cool down the mango kheera. Add the cardamom powder and mix..
  7. Refrigerate to cool the mango kheera and serve, garnishing with dry fruits and raisins. Thanks.
