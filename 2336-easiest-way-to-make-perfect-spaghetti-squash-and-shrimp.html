Spaghetti Squash and Shrimp step by step

  1. Cut squash and scrape out insides..
  2. Place squash with 1 cup water in instant pot for 6 minutes then quick release or can bake at 350 with 1 cup water until done..
  3. While squash is cooking sauté the garlic in butter. When it becomes fragrant add rest of the ingredients. Sauté until shrimp is done..
  4. Add squash to the shrimp and enjoy!.
