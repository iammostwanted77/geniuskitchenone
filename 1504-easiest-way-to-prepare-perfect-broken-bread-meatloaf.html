Broken Bread Meatloaf instructions

  1. Preheat oven 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Boil water..
  2. Mix the spices.
  3. Break the bread into pieces and soak in milk. Let it soak for as long as it takes to mix the meatloaf ingredients..
  4. Mix the meat, crackercrumbs, eggs, crackers, and spices..
  5. Add the soaked broken bread..
  6. Mix well..
  7. Form a loaf.
  8. Dent the top with a wooden spoon. If you are not familiar with the dent it's just like trough..
  9. The King Hawaiian Pineapple Barbecue Sauce.
  10. Coat the top with the sauce..
  11. Fill the dent with the rest of the barbecue sauce..
  12. Take and put the loaf pan into a pan add the boiling water to the pan around the loaf pan..
  13. Bake in oven for 1-1/4 hours to 1-1/2 hours.
  14. Let rest for 10 minutes..
  15. Cut the loaf into desired portions..
  16. Serve I hope you enjoy!.
