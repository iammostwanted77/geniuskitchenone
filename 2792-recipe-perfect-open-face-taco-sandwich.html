Open Face Taco Sandwich step by step

  1. In a large pot brown your ground beef, drain off any fat.....
  2. Add your chopped green pepper, can of green chiles and garlic to ground beef, sautÃ© until veggies are tender.....
  3. Next add your taco seasoning and required amount of water, cook and stir until thickened....Remove from heat when cooked thru....Then add your can of refried beans to cooked taco mixture, mix all well.....
  4. Take your bakery rolls and slice them in half, then place them on a large baking sheet, cut sides up......
  5. Place bread halves in oven under broiler to lightly toast them.....
  6. Spoon taco mix onto each half of rolls.....
  7. Place slice of red onion on top of taco halves.....
  8. Add some shredded Mexican cheese on top of halves....
