Best Deviled Eggs instructions

  1. Boil water in Medium Pot with salt (this keeps eggs from cracking). Gently place each egg in pot one at a time and continue to boil for exactly 10 minutes.
  2. Let eggs cool for about 10 minutes in refrigerator..
  3. Peel the eggs. Cut eggs in half vertical way (top to bottom down the center).
  4. Gently without breaking the eggs, pop out the yolks and place in a small bowl. Arrange the half eggs on plate..
  5. Mix the yolk until smooth and clump free. Add Mayo, Mustard, Relish, salt and pepper. Mix well until smooth..
  6. Spoon the mixture in each egg cavitity..
  7. Sprinkle with paprika! :).
