Ingredients of Super Easy and Delicious Alfredo Sauce

  1. It's 1/2 C of Unsalted Butter.
  2. It's 8 oz of Cream Cheese (softened).
  3. It's 3 tsp of Minced Garlic or 2 tsp Garlic Powder.
  4. It's 2 C of Milk.
  5. You need 6 oz of Grated Parmesan Cheese.
  6. You need 1/4 tsp of Ground Black Pepper.

Once you've mastered this easy Alfredo sauce recipe, use it to make any number of delicious pasta dishes. It's great with chicken and shrimp (especially when they're seasoned with Cajun seasoning). You can also add any number of vegetables, like spinach or kale, broccoli, spring peas, asparagus, mushrooms. This easy alfredo sauce makes enough for one pound of pasta, if you like your pasta heavily dressed.

Super Easy and Delicious Alfredo Sauce step by step

  1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat..
  2. Add cream cheese and garlic, stirring with wire whisk until smooth..
  3. Add milk, a little at a time, whisking to smooth out lumps..
  4. Stir in Parmesan and pepper..
  5. Remove from heat when sauce reaches desired consistency..
  6. Sauce will thicken rapidly, thin with milk if cooked too long..
  7. Keep the heat at medium to avoid scorching the sauce..
