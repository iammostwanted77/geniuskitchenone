Deviled Eggs for devilish people step by step

  1. Take halved, scooped eggs and place on decorative plate..
  2. Mix mayonaise, sweet relish, paprika and black pepper into hard boiled egg yolks till smooth..
  3. Taste filling. Add additional relish for sweeter, black pepper or paprika for spicier eggs..
  4. Fill hard boiled egg whites with filling..
  5. Chill in fridge till ready to serve..
