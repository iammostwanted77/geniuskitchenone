Ingredients of Chocolate peanut butter cookies

  1. It's 3/4 cup of extra crunchy jif pb.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of oil.
  3. It's 1 1/4 cup of firmly packed brown sugar.
  4. You need 3 tbsp of milk.
  5. You need 1 tbsp of vanilla extract.
  6. Prepare 2 of shakes of tumeric.
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp of ground cinnamon.
  8. You need 1/2 cup of bakers cocoa.
  9. It's 1 of egg.
  10. You need 1 3/4 cup of flour.
  11. You need 3/4 tsp of baking soda.

Chocolate peanut butter cookies step by step

  1. Heat oven at350'.
  2. Combine pb,oil,brown sugar milk and vanilla in lg bowl beat until well blended.add in egg and mix together..
  3. Add in cinnamon sugar and chocolate mix together add in flour and baking soda mix together.
  4. Roll dough into small balls and flatten with sugar coated fork bake for7 minutes and when done let cool or eat warm with milk.
