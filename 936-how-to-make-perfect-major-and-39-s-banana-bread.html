Ingredients of Major's banana bread

  1. It's 1 cup of brown sugar.
  2. You need 1/2 cup of melted butter.
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup of milk.
  4. You need 2 of eggs.
  5. Prepare 4 of ripe bananas.
  6. Prepare 2 cup of bisquick.

Major's banana bread step by step

  1. preheat oven to 350°.
  2. cream together brown sugar, butter, milk and eggs.
  3. mix in bisquick.
  4. in pieces add bananas 1 at a time until incorporated.
  5. pour batter into greased bread pan or baking dish of choice (i use an 8x10 casserole because i dont have a bread pan).
  6. bake at 350° for 45-55 minutes.
  7. remove from oven, cool for 5 minutes for easier removal from pan.
