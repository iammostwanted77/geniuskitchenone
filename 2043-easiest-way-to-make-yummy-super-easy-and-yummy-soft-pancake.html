Ingredients of Super easy and yummy soft pancake

  1. You need 1 of egg.
  2. Prepare 1 cup of all-purpose flour.
  3. You need 1 cup of water (prepare a bit more).
  4. Prepare 3 spoons of sugar.
  5. It's 1/2 tbs of baking powder.
  6. It's 2 drops of vanilla extract.
  7. It's 1 pinch of salt.

Super easy and yummy soft pancake step by step

  1. Mix baking powder and flour in a cup..
  2. Mix egg and salt until it bubbles, and add sugar. Mix until many bubbles comes out and all sugar is dissolved..
  3. Add vanilla extract and sift in flour little by little, as well as water until the batter is not too thick and well combined..
  4. Cook on pan, when you see a little brown on the edge, apply your favorite jam on it quickly..
  5. Lift out the pancake with jam inside and it's ready to serve..
