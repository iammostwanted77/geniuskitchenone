Ingredients of Spicy Potato Sandwich

  1. It's 2 of Potatoes, boiled.
  2. It's 1 of Capsicum, finely chopped.
  3. Prepare 1 of Onion, finely chopped.
  4. Prepare 1 of Green Chili, finely chopped.
  5. It's 1/2 Cup of Semolina.
  6. You need 1/4 Cup of Curd.
  7. Prepare of Salt, as per taste.
  8. You need 1/4 tbsp of Baking Powder.
  9. Prepare 1/4 tbsp of Garam Masala.
  10. You need 1/4 tbsp of Aamchur Powder.
  11. You need 1/4 tbsp of Red Chili Powder.
  12. It's of Oil/ Butter.
  13. Prepare of Cheddar Cheese, Grated.

Spicy Potato Sandwich instructions

  1. Add semolina, curd, baking powder and salt in a mixing bowl. Mix well..
  2. Add a little water to make a smooth paste..
  3. Cover and keep aside for 10 minutes. This way semolina will absorb all the moisture..
  4. To make potato filling, grate the boiled potatoes..
  5. Add chopped onion and green chili, capsicum, garam masala, aamchur powder, salt and red chili powder. Mix well..
  6. Add grated cheese to this filling and combine everything..
  7. Now, add a little bit of water in the semolina paste to make a batter like Dosa..
  8. Heat some oil in pan..
  9. When the oil is hot enough, pour some semolina batter and spread with a spoon..
  10. Place the potato stuffing at the center..
  11. Pour the batter on the top of potato stuffing spreading evenly. The semolina dosa should get covered with the batter..
  12. Cover with the lid and let it cook on low flame for few minutes..
  13. After few minutes, flip the sandwich and let it cook from another side..
  14. Don't forget to pour few drops of oil..
  15. Cook till both the sides are golden brown..
  16. Crispy, spicy potato tawa sandwich is ready to serve..
  17. Serve with Tomato Ketchup Or Green Chutney..
