Ingredients of Creamy White Chicken Chili - Slow Cooker

  1. Prepare 1 of large boneless, skinless chicken breast.
  2. It's 1/2 of yellow onion, diced.
  3. It's 3 cloves of garlic, minced.
  4. It's 1 of jalapeno, diced (seeds removed for non-spicy).
  5. It's 1 can (4 oz.) of diced green chiles.
  6. You need 2 cans (15 oz.) of white beans, drained and rinsed.
  7. You need 2 cups of unsalted chicken broth.
  8. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp. of ground cumin.
  9. It's 1 tsp. of salt.
  10. You need 1/2 tsp. of dried oregano.
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp. of pepper.
  12. You need 1/2 tsp. of ground coriander.
  13. Prepare 4 oz. of brick style cream cheese, cut into cubes.
  14. Prepare of Juice from 1/2 lime.

Creamy White Chicken Chili - Slow Cooker instructions

  1. Grease your slow cooker (a 4 or 6 qt size is good for this recipe). Place all ingredients in EXCEPT for the lime juice (which will get added in at the end). Place the lid on and cook on low heat for 6-7 hours, or until the chicken is fall apart tender..
  2. After cook time is up, remove the chicken from the slow cooker and shred it up. Stir it back into the chili along with the lime juice..
  3. Add your favorite toppings and enjoy! To reheat leftovers, just place the amount you want reheated into a small pot over medium heat for 5-10 minutes, until completely heated through..
