Ingredients of Banana bread

  1. Prepare 1/2 of cup/ 125g of soft butter.
  2. It's 3/4 cup of brown sugar.
  3. You need 2 of eggs.
  4. Prepare 2 cups of AP flour (wheat).
  5. Prepare 3 tsp of baking powder.
  6. You need 1/4 tsp of salt.
  7. It's 3 of large ripe bananas.

Banana bread instructions

  1. Combine butter, sugar and eggs. Sufficient to use a regular spatula to mix.
  2. Sift in flour, baking powder and salt.
  3. Mash bananas with a fork and add it in.
  4. Bake at 180 degC for 1 hour.
  5. Let it cool before cutting it. Best to let it cool on a wire mesh so that the base dries as well..
