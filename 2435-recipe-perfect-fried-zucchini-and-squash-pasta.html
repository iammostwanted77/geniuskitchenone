Ingredients of Fried zucchini and squash pasta

  1. Prepare 1 of green zucchini.
  2. It's 1 of yellow squash.
  3. It's 1 packages of spaghetti noodles.
  4. It's 2 of Jars spaghetti sauce.
  5. You need 1 packages of mozzarella cheese.
  6. You need 1 of egg.
  7. It's 1/4 cup of milk.
  8. It's 1 cup of flour.
  9. You need 1 of basil.
  10. Prepare 1 of salt.
  11. Prepare 1 of thyme.
  12. Prepare 1 of pepper.
  13. Prepare 1 of garlic powder.
  14. Prepare 1 of vegetable oil.

Yellow squash and zucchini are simmered with onion, tomatoes and green chile peppers. This is an extremely easy and fast side dish. It is even good served over pasta. In a large saucepan, combine squash, zucchini, tomatoes with chiles, onion, salt and garlic powder.

Fried zucchini and squash pasta step by step

  1. Cook and Drain noodles. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. In med. bowl beat milk and egg together and set aside..
  3. On a plate combine flour and seasonings and mix together..
  4. Cut up zucchini and squash in chips and dip in the egg mixture then coat in the flour mixture. Repeat until all pieces are coated..
  5. In skillet heat oil. Fry zucchini and squash chips until crispy on both sides. Repeat until gone..
  6. Combine one jar of sauce with the noodles and pour into a baking dish..
  7. Pour second jar of sauce over the top and layer with alternating zucchini and squash chips. Top with cheese and bake until melted..
