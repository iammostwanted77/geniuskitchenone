Ingredients of Chocolate Chip, Peanut Butter Cookies

  1. It's 2 1/4 cup of all purpose flour.
  2. It's 3/4 cup of packed brown sugar.
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup of sugar.
  4. You need 2 stick of butter, softened.
  5. Prepare 2 large of eggs.
  6. Prepare 2 cup of chocolate chips.
  7. You need 1 cup of peanut butter.
  8. It's 1 tsp of salt.
  9. Prepare 1 tsp of baking soda.
  10. You need 1 tsp of vanilla extract.

Chocolate Chip, Peanut Butter Cookies step by step

  1. preheat oven to 350Â°.
  2. in a bowl, combine flour, brown sugar, sugar, baking soda, salt, and vanilla extract. Stir together good..
  3. Then, beat together flour mixture with butter and eggs..
  4. Once you have beaten the eggs and butter into the mixture and everything is moist, add chocolate chips and peanut butter. Mix together well with a wooden spoon..
  5. get a teaspoon full of cookie dough and work them into small balls into the palm of the hand and place on a sprayed cookie sheet..
