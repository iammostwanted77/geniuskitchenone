Ingredients of Italian spiced chicken stir fry

  1. Prepare 2 of large, skinless, boneless chicken breasts, diced.
  2. It's 1 of large yellow squash or zucchini, ribbon cut or cut into thin sticks.
  3. You need 6 of baby bello mushrooms, sliced.
  4. It's 1 of can of diced no salt added tomatoes, drained.
  5. It's 1 of bunch fresh baby spinach leaves.
  6. Prepare 1 oz of spaghetti noodles, I like to mix spinach and reg noodles.
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup of canola or olive oil.
  8. It's 1 of salt to taste.
  9. You need 1 of crushed red pepper flakes, to taste.
  10. Prepare 1 of onion powder, to taste.
  11. You need 1 of Italian seasoning, to taste.
  12. You need 1 of dried parsley, to taste.
  13. Prepare 1 of garlic powder, to taste.
  14. Prepare 1 of ancho chili powder, to taste.
  15. Prepare 1 of ground black pepper, to taste.
  16. It's 1 of parmesan cheese, grated, to taste.

Italian spiced chicken stir fry instructions

  1. Heat oil in either a wok or large pan with cover. Med to med-high, don't let it start to smoke..
  2. Once oil is hot carefully add diced chicken. Then add the herbs and spices. Stir around..
  3. While chicken cooks, prepare noodles in another pot..
  4. Once the chicken is half way cooked add squash, add more spices, if you wish. Stir together and cover for a few minutes..
  5. When squash is mostly cooked add the tomatoes stir together and cover again..
  6. Let cook, covered, for several more minutes stirring occasionally then add mushrooms. Stir together..
  7. Carefully, drain water from noodles and add to wok, stirring them in..
  8. Just before you serve add the spinach leaves. Stir and let cook for just another minute or two so they just begin to wilt..
  9. Turn off stove and add parmesan, stir and serve..
